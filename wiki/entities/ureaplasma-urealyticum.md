# Ureaplasma urealyticum

*See also: [[ureaplasma]] (genus page with full biology and metal dependency profile)*

*Ureaplasma urealyticum* is one of two human-pathogenic Ureaplasma species (alongside *U. parvum*). Like all Ureaplasma, it depends on nickel-dependent urease for energy generation.

## Key Associations

- Chronic prostatitis: Detected in prostatic secretions of men with chronic prostatitis [[magri-2018-multidisciplinary-prostatitis]].
- Male infertility: Associated with impaired sperm quality through ammonia-mediated toxicity [[magill-2023-male-infertility-human-microbiome]].
