# Lactobacillaceae

Lactobacillaceae is the family containing the genus [[lactobacillus]] and related genera. It is the dominant family in the healthy vaginal microbiome and a significant family in the gut. Family-level depletion is reported across schizophrenia [[szeligowski-2020-gut-microbiome-schizophrenia-review]] [[li-2024-alterations-gut-microbiota-schizophrenia-vote-counting]], endometriosis [[khan-2016-intrauterine-microbial-colonization-endometriosis]], male reproductive dysfunction [[lv-2024-gut-microbiota-male-reproductive-function-review]], and endometrial cancer [[yang-2025-gut-microbiota-endometrial-cancer-mr]].

For species-level detail, see [[lactobacillus]], [[lactobacillus-reuteri]], [[lactobacillus-acidophilus]], [[lactobacillus-gasseri]], [[lactobacillus-jensenii]].
