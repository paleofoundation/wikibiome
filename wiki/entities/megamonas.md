## Overview

**Megamonas** is a genus of obligate anaerobic, Gram-negative bacteria in the family Veillonellaceae (phylum Firmicutes). It is a common member of the human gut microbiota, particularly in Asian populations, and is involved in carbohydrate fermentation and short-chain fatty acid (SCFA) production.
