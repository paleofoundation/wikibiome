# Alloscardovia

Alloscardovia is a genus of anaerobic, Gram-positive bacteria in the family Bifidobacteriaceae (phylum Actinobacteria). The primary species is *A. omnicolens*, which colonizes the human urogenital tract. In the WikiBiome vault, Alloscardovia appears exclusively in endometriosis vaginal microbiome studies.

## Endometriosis Associations

- Part of the vaginal microbiome shifts observed in endometriosis patients [[miyashira-2022-microbiome-and-endometriosis]] [[macsharry-2024-endometriosis-vaginal-microbiota-n-glycome]].
- Associated with the vaginal microbiome in chronic pelvic pain and endometriosis [[chao-2021-vaginal-microbiome-chronic-pelvic-pain-endometriosis]].
