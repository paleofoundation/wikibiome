# Paraprevotella

Paraprevotella is a genus of obligate anaerobic, Gram-negative bacteria in the family Prevotellaceae, related to [[prevotella]]. It is a gut commensal with emerging associations across neuropsychiatric and reproductive conditions.

- Schizophrenia: Altered in gut-brain axis studies [[ghorbani-2024-gut-microbiome-dopamine-serotonin-bdnf-schizophrenia]].
- Endometriosis: Depleted in gut and stool microbiota [[svensson-2021-endometriosis-gut-microbiota-associations]] [[hicks-2025-oral-vaginal-stool-microbial-signatures-endometriosis]].
- CVD: MR causal associations [[dai-2024-bidirectional-mr-gut-microbiota-cvd]].
- Panic disorder: Depleted in perimenopausal panic disorder [[lin-2023-gut-microbiota-perimenopausal-panic-disorder]].
