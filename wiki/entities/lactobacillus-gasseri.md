# Lactobacillus gasseri

*Lactobacillus gasseri* is a vaginal and gut commensal that defines Community State Type II (CST-II) in the vaginal microbiome. It is a common probiotic species with anti-obesity properties in clinical trials.

## Vaginal Ecology

- Dominates CST-II vaginal microbiomes — less protective than *L. crispatus* (CST-I) but more protective than *L. iners* (CST-III).
- Part of vaginal microbiome shifts in endometriosis [[uzuner-2023-bidirectional-relationship-endometriosis-microbiome]] [[macsharry-2024-endometriosis-vaginal-microbiota-n-glycome]].
- Preterm birth associations [[pruski-2021-desi-ms-vaginal-microbiome-preterm-birth]].
- Ovarian cancer microbiome context [[asangba-2023-microbiome-ovarian-cancer-diagnostic-prognostic]].

## Clinical Evidence

- Schizophrenia: Component of synbiotic for metabolic syndrome in schizophrenia RCT [[basafa-roodi-2024-synbiotic-metabolic-syndrome-schizophrenia-rct]].
- FMT: Component of reproductive tract FMT protocols [[quaranta-2019-fmt-female-reproductive-tract-diseases]].
