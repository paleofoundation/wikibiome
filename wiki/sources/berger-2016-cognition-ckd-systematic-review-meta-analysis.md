# Cognition in CKD: Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients perform worse on multiple cognitive domains
- Cognitive changes occur early in CKD and decline at different rates
- Language scores dropped significantly when eGFR less than 45

## Methods & Findings

Systematic review and meta-analysis of 44 studies (51,575 participants). Standardized mean differences across 8 cognitive domains.
