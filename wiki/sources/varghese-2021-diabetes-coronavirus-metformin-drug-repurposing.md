# Key Claims

- **Diabetic individuals at increased risk** of COVID-19 complications, ICU admissions, and mortality
- **Metformin treatment correlated with significant reduction** in disease severity and mortality in COVID-19 patients
- **Metformin has antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, immunomodulatory, and antiviral capabilities** beyond glucose-lowering
- **Molecular mechanisms described** for metformin's cardiopulmonary and vascular protection in COVID-19

# Methods & Findings

Review of molecular mechanisms for metformin benefit in COVID-19 diabetic patients:

- Insulin administration alone in diabetic COVID-19 patients was associated with adverse outcomes
- Metformin's pleiotropic effects provide multi-system protection
- Scientific basis laid out for drug repurposing of metformin
