# Zhou 2023 — Probiotics Prevent NEC in VLBW (Network Meta-Analysis)

## Key Findings

- **Bovine lactoferrin combined with *L. rhamnosus GG*** ranks first for NEC prevention in very-low-birth-weight infants (SUCRA = 95.7%).
- **Four-strain combination** (*L. rhamnosus + L. plantarum + L. casei + B. lactis*) ranks second (SUCRA = 89.4%).
- **Lactoferrin mechanisms:** iron sequestration (nutritional immunity, primitive 2/8), direct antimicrobial action, immune modulation — layered synergy with probiotic colonization.
- Across 27 RCTs and 9,501 infants, combination regimens consistently outperform single-strain probiotics.

## Methods

Network meta-analysis of 27 randomized controlled trials. Outcome: NEC incidence in VLBW preterm infants. SUCRA probability rankings.
