# Cervical Cancer, HPV, and the Vaginal Microbiome: A Mega-Analysis

## Key Claims

- CC patients exhibited significantly higher alpha diversity and shift from Lactobacillus-dominant to polymicrobial vaginal microbiome
- Increased Porphyromonadaceae, Campylobacter ureolyticus, Peptococcus niger, Anaerococcus obesiensis
- Functional enrichment in chronic inflammation, fatty acid biosynthesis, amino acid metabolism, cellular proliferation
