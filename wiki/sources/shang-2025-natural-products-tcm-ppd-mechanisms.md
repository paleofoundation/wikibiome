# Key Claims

- **PPD is more prevalent in developing countries** (15.0%) compared to developed countries (12.0%)
- Conventional antidepressants have drawbacks including delayed onset, side effects, and low patient tolerance; **15-30% of patients discontinue** medication prematurely
- **TCM formulas** exert effects via synergistic actions of diverse metabolites on multiple pathways simultaneously
- **Neuroinflammation** is a key pathological mechanism; pregnancy-related stress increases IL-1a, IL-6, TNF-alpha, and IFN-gamma
- **137 articles** included in systematic analysis of natural product and TCM mechanisms in PPD

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Systematic review following PRISMA guidelines; PubMed, Web of Science, ScienceDirect searched January 2000-April 2024
- **Key mechanisms**: Neuroinflammation, aberrant neuroplasticity, MGB axis dysfunction, neuroendocrine abnormalities
- **Key TCM formulas**: 919 syrup and other botanical drug combinations
