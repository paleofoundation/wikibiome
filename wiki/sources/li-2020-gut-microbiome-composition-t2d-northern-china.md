# Key Claims

- **Significant decrease in gut microbiota diversity** in diabetic vs healthy Chinese individuals
- **Butyrate-producing bacteria (Bifidobacterium, Akkermansia) significantly decreased** in diabetic patients
- **Dorea was significantly increased** in T2DM individuals and negatively correlated with butyrate-producing bacteria
- **Functional analysis revealed** increased butyrate production via transferases and degradation of amino acids correlated with T2DM
- **Gut microbiota-based classifiers** can identify high-risk individuals for T2D

# Methods & Findings

16S rRNA pyrosequencing of V4V5 region from cohort of healthy and diabetic Chinese individuals:

- Dorea increase is a previously unappreciated finding in T2DM
- Strong correlations between microbial composition and metabolic functions
- Classifier potential for identifying pre-diabetic individuals
