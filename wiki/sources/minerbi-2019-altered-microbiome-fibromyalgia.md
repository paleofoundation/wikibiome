# Minerbi 2019 — Altered Microbiome Composition in Fibromyalgia

## Key Findings

- **19 species** were significantly differentially abundant between FM patients and controls, with abundances correlating with **pain severity, fatigue, and cognitive symptoms**.
- **Serum SCFA signature:** butyric acid *elevated* in FM (counter-intuitive), propionic and isobutyric *reduced*.
- **Machine-learning classifiers** using bacterial profiles distinguished FM from controls with **AUC up to 87.8%**.
- Landmark early case-control establishing the FM microbiome signature.

## Methods

Case-control at McGill. 77 FM patients + 79 household/matched controls. Shotgun metagenomics + targeted serum SCFA. Multiple confounder adjustment (diet, medications, BMI).

## Contradictions / Tensions

- Elevated serum butyrate in FM contrasts with depleted butyrate-producer taxa in meta-analysis ([[goudman-2024-gut-dysbiosis-chronic-pain-meta-analysis]]); reconcile as altered butyrate uptake or production kinetics rather than simple deficit.

## Open Questions

- Replication in larger, geographically diverse cohorts.
- Causal direction (addressed partially by MR studies).
