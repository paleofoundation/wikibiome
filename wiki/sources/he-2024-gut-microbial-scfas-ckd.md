# Gut Microbial Metabolites SCFAs and CKD

## Key Claims

- SCFAs play versatile role in influencing host energy homeostasis, immune function, and intermicrobial signaling
- SCFAs emerge as pivotal risk factors in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] development and prognosis
- SCFAs may serve as biomarkers for CKD progression and as adjunctive therapeutic strategies

## Methods & Findings

Review elucidating impact of gut microbial metabolites SCFAs on CKD.
