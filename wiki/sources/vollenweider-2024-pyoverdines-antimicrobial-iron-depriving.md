## Key Findings

- **320 natural pyoverdine variants screened** — specific structural variants potently inhibit [[acinetobacter-baumannii]], [[klebsiella-pneumoniae]], and [[staphylococcus-aureus]] by competitive [[iron]] deprivation
- **Mechanism is iron-dependent with low host toxicity** — pyoverdines sequester iron from pathogens without disrupting host iron metabolism at effective concentrations
- **Reduced resistance evolution potential** compared to conventional antibiotics, because the target (iron acquisition) is a fundamental metabolic requirement rather than a single enzyme
