# Infectious Spondylitis in a Patient with Chronic Kidney Disease

## Key Claims

- First reported case of Campylobacter fetus subsp. testudinum spondylitis
- Identified via 16S rRNA sequencing in a patient with ESRD
- Underlying [[chronic-kidney-disease]]/ESRD may predispose to opportunistic infections

## Methods & Findings

Case report of 81-year-old male with ESRD; C. fetus identified by 16S rRNA sequencing of blood cultures.
