# Key Claims

- **Bile acid metabolism is altered in T1D** with implications for gut barrier function and immune regulation
- **Calprotectin elevation in T1D** indicates intestinal inflammation and barrier dysfunction
- **SCFA alterations (decreased butyrate and propionate)** in T1D reflect loss of beneficial fermentative bacteria
