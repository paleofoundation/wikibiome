# The OC-Associated Microbiome and the Tumor's Inflammatory Microenvironment

## Key Claims

- Microbiome alterations and associated inflammatory changes in OC provide theoretical support for microbiome-targeted antitumor therapies
- Propionibacterium acnes, upward migration of pathogens, and gut dysbiosis implicated in OC development
- Review covers gut, vaginal, and peritoneal compartment microbiome changes
