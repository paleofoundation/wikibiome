# Key Claims

- **1,752 articles** addressing inflammation and ASD published, with consistent upward trend
- **"Children"** was most frequently occurring keyword, followed by "oxidative stress" and "brain"
- **Keyword burst analysis** revealed notable periods of focus on IBD, cytokine production, neurodevelopmental disorders, and microbiota
- **Brain Behavior and Immunity** identified as the most prominent journal in this field
- University of California System was the most productive institution
