# Key Claims

- **Intestinal microbiome directly modulates immune system** through multiple pathways relevant to T1D
- **Treg/Th17 balance** is critically influenced by gut microbiome composition
- **Autoimmune tolerance breakdown** in T1D involves microbiome-immune axis disruption
- **Microbiome-based immunomodulation** could prevent or delay T1D onset
