## Key Findings

- **Combined dietary fiber and probiotic intervention** significantly improved constipation in CP children: defecation frequency rose from 2.17 to 3.61 per week at 6 months.
- **Stool consistency normalized**: Bristol Stool Scale score improved from 1.68 (hard/lumpy) to 3.71 (normal) over the study period.
- **Butyrate-producing genera** ([[bacteroides]], [[lachnospiraceae]], [[faecalibacterium-prausnitzii]]) increased at 1 month but declined by 6 months, suggesting the intervention effect on microbiome composition may not be durable without ongoing support.
- [[lactobacillus]] supplementation contributed to initial microbiome shifts.
