## Key Findings

- Prospective cohort at Children's Hospital of Chongqing Medical University (Dec 2020–Dec 2021). 168 preterm infants presenting with abdominal distension or hematochezia; 22 diagnosed with NEC (Bell stage ≥2) and 21 with FPIAP (confirmed by oral food challenge). Stool samples collected within 24h of diagnosis.
- **Phylum level**: NEC had higher **Actinobacteriota** and lower **Bacteroidota** than FPIAP (p<0.05). Firmicutes and Proteobacteria similar.
- **Genus level**: NEC significantly enriched in **Halomonas, Acinetobacter, Bifidobacterium, Stenotrophomonas** (vs FPIAP). FPIAP enriched in Enterobacteriaceae (unclassified), *Enterococcus*, *Escherichia-Shigella*, *Klebsiella*, *Streptococcus*.
- **SCFAs** (GC-MS): NEC had significantly **lower acetic, propionic, butyric, isovaleric, and total SCFAs**, and **higher hexanoic acid** vs FPIAP (p<0.05). Valeric acid not different.
- ROC-AUC for genus-level discrimination: Actinobacteriota 0.685, Bacteroidota 0.787, Halomonas 0.807, Acinetobacter 0.877, Stenotrophomonas 0.753, Bifidobacterium 0.781. SCFAs also showed moderate diagnostic power.
- NEC diagnosis age 11.6 days (IQR 6.8–16.0), FPIAP 15.2 days (IQR 11.0–22.0). 91% of FPIAP infants exclusively breastfed vs 9% of NEC — feeding pattern is a major confounder.

## Relevance to WikiBiome

- Supports [[necrotizing-enterocolitis]] disease entity with distinct vs allergic (FPIAP) microbial/SCFA profile.
- Adds *Halomonas*, *Acinetobacter*, *Stenotrophomonas* as NEC-enriched taxa — nosocomial/environmental opportunists that thrive in preterm NICU ecology.
- Reinforces [[butyrate]] / [[short-chain-fatty-acids]] concept pages: SCFA depletion in NEC reflects loss of commensal fermentation capacity.
- Adds a comparative dimension to the NEC narrative — bloody stool is not diagnostic alone; the microbiome separates the two etiologies.

## Relevance to Cureva

- Differential diagnosis: combining gut microbiota (Halomonas, Acinetobacter) with fecal SCFA panel can assist NEC vs FPIAP differentiation in preterm infants presenting with GI bleeding.
- NEC signature: supports SCFA-depleted ecological state as a confidence layer.
- Intervention implication: butyrate/SCFA restoration strategies (fiber, butyrate-producing probiotics) are differentially relevant for NEC vs FPIAP.

## Contradictions / Tensions

- Bifidobacterium enrichment in NEC is counter to typical expectation (Bifido is usually commensal/protective) — likely driven by differential feeding (breastmilk vs CM-formula) between groups.
- FPIAP population was largely breastfed, introducing a feeding confounder that cannot be fully adjusted.

## Open Questions

- Longitudinal SCFA trajectory pre-NEC onset vs pre-FPIAP.
- Mechanistic role of Halomonas (halophilic Gammaproteobacteria) in NEC — an unusual finding requiring replication.
