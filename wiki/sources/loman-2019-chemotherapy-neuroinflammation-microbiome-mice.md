# Chemotherapy-Induced Neuroinflammation and Disrupted Colonic/Bacterial Homeostasis

## Key Claims

- Paclitaxel concurrently affects gut microbiome, colonic tissue integrity, microglia activation, and fatigue
- Colonic histology and bacterial community changes more predictive of brain/behavioral responses than microbiome changes alone
- Novel relationship identified between colonic tissue integrity and behavioral responses via brain-gut-microbiota axis
