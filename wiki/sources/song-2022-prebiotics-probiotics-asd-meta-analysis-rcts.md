# Key Claims

- **Probiotics and prebiotics did not significantly improve** the severity of ASD patients overall
- **GI problems and comorbid psychopathology** in ASD were also not significantly improved
- **Results are contradictory** to previous literature reviews
- **Relatively few clinical controlled trials** available for inclusion
- More RCTs with larger populations needed for robust conclusions

# Methods & Findings

**Systematic review and meta-analysis** of RCTs only (not observational studies).

- Searched PubMed, Web of Science, Embase, and Cochrane Library
- Included only clinical controlled trials of probiotics and prebiotics in ASD
- Assessed overall ASD severity, GI symptom severity, and comorbid psychopathology
- Found no significant improvement across these outcomes
- Highlighted gap between observational evidence and RCT evidence

# Relevance to ASD Framework

- **Important null finding** -- RCT evidence does not yet support probiotics/prebiotics for ASD symptom improvement
- **Methodological rigor** matters -- observational studies and reviews may overstate probiotic benefits
- **Need for better-designed trials** with standardized outcomes and larger samples
