# Nutritional Immunity and Metal Intoxication in Pathogenic Neisseria

## Key Findings

- **Dual metal stress**: The host deploys BOTH metal starvation ([[calprotectin]]-mediated Zn/Mn sequestration) AND metal intoxication (macrophage zinc/copper poisoning) against *Neisseria* — pathogens must survive both extremes simultaneously [[branch-2022-nutritional-immunity-metal-intoxication-neisseria]], expert-opinion.
- **Complete nutritional immunity picture**: Most WikiBiome pages describe nutritional immunity as metal restriction. This paper adds the second half — metal intoxication. The host uses BOTH too-little AND too-much metal as weapons.
