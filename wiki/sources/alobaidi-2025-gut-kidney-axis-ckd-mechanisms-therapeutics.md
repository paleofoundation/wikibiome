# The Gut-Kidney Axis in CKD: Mechanisms and Therapeutics

## Key Claims

- Gut-kidney axis central to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] pathogenesis via microbial [[dysbiosis]] and altered metabolite production
- Harmful metabolites (indoxyl sulfate, p-cresyl sulfate, TMAO) promote inflammation and fibrosis
- Loss of protective SCFAs impairs barrier integrity and immune regulation
- Microbiome-targeted interventions (diet, probiotics, synbiotics, FMT, oral adsorbents) critically evaluated

## Methods & Findings

Comprehensive review integrating mechanistic, translational, clinical, and therapeutic perspectives on the gut-kidney axis.
