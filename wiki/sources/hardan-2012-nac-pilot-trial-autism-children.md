# Key Claims

- **NAC at 900mg daily** resulted in significant improvements on ABC irritability subscale (F=6.80, p<0.01, d=0.96)
- **31 males and 2 females** (3.2-10.7 years) randomized; follow-up data on 14 NAC and 15 placebo subjects
- **NAC was well tolerated** with limited side effects
- **NAC is a glutamatergic modulator and antioxidant** that may address both excitotoxicity and oxidative stress in ASD
- Pilot data supports usefulness of NAC for treating irritability in children with autistic disorder
