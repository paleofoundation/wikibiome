# Key Claims

- **Microbiome pharmacology** aims to restore microbial homeostasis, introduce beneficial microbial functions, or modulate existing microbial activities
- **Diverse strategies** reviewed from FMT to novel small-molecule modulators and next-generation probiotics
- **Microbiome influences drug metabolism** with implications for therapeutic efficacy
- **Clinical applications** discussed in GI disorders, neurological conditions, and oncology
- **Persistent challenges** highlighted alongside promising future directions

# Methods & Findings

**Review** synthesizing developments from 2023 to early 2025 in microbiome-targeted interventions.

- Covered FMT, next-generation probiotics, and novel small-molecule modulators
- Discussed microbiome influence on drug metabolism and bioavailability
- Reviewed applications in GI, neurological, and oncological conditions
- Highlighted precision medicine approaches based on individual microbiome profiles

# Relevance to ASD Framework

- **Microbiome-targeted therapies** as emerging treatment paradigm for ASD
- **Drug-microbiome interactions** relevant to ASD pharmacotherapy
- **Next-generation probiotics** as more targeted approach than conventional probiotics
