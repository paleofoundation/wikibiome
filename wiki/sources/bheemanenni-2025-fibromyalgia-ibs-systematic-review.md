# Bheemanenni 2025 — Fibromyalgia and IBS (Systematic Review)

## Key Findings

- **High comorbidity:** FM-IBS overlap ranges from **28% to 59%** across 10 included studies.
- **Shared microbial signature:** depleted Bifidobacteria and Lachnospiraceae in both conditions.
- **Shared mechanism:** altered serotonin/tryptophan metabolism via the gut–brain axis.
- *Pseudomonas aeruginosa* reported in subset of both-condition populations — potential shared pathobiont.

## Methods

Systematic review of 10 clinical and microbiome studies examining FM-IBS comorbidity and shared gut microbial features.
