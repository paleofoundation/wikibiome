# Gut Microbiota Therapy for CKD

## Key Claims

- Prebiotics (resistant starch) lower uremic toxins and reduce inflammation
- Low-protein and gluten-free diets modulate microbial diversity and improve renal biomarkers
- FMT stabilizes creatinine levels and shifts gut microbiota toward beneficial taxa
- Emerging phage therapy and AI-driven multi-omics hold significant promise for [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
