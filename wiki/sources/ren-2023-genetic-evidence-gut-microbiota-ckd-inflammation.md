# Genetic Evidence Supporting the Causal Role of Gut Microbiota in CKD

## Key Claims

- Protective factors for [[chronic-kidney-disease]] include Lachnospiraceae, Eubacterium eligens, Intestinimonas, Streptococcus
- LachnospiraceaeUCG010 is a risk factor for CKD
- Higher Desulfovibrio abundance correlated with higher eGFR
- Multiple taxa associated with inflammatory markers (CRP, UACR)

## Methods & Findings

Bilateral two-sample MR using GWAS data for gut microbiota, CKD, eGFR, CRP, and UACR.
