# Key Claims

- **Peripartum mood and anxiety disorders (PMADs)** affect 15-20% of peripartum women
- **L. rhamnosus HN001** treatment from first day of pregnancy through postpartum day 10 in rats altered postpartum anxiety and cortical monoamines
- Probiotic effects on anxiety and caregiving behaviors were examined against a background of chronic variable pregnancy stress
- Study provides preclinical evidence for [[gut-brain-axis]] modulation of postpartum affective behavior

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Rodent model; female rats received probiotic in drinking water from pregnancy day 1 through postpartum day 10; half underwent chronic variable pregnancy stress
- **Key measures**: Postpartum affective and caregiving behaviors, cortical monoamines, gut microbiome composition
