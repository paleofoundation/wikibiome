# Compositional and Functional Imbalance of Gut Microbiota in CKD

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] subjects show significantly reduced alpha diversity and disrupted microbial composition
- Klebsiella significantly enriched in CKD while Akkermansia higher in healthy controls
- Heterogeneity in microbial signatures across CKD subtypes (MN, IgAN, MCD, IRI)
- Gut microbial diagnostic markers can accurately discriminate between CKD subtypes

## Methods & Findings

Cross-sectional study of 94 CKD patients and 54 healthy controls. 16S rRNA sequencing.
