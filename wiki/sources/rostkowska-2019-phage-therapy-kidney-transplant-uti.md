# Phage Therapy for Recurrent UTI in Kidney Transplant

## Key Claims

- Phage therapy used as last-resort treatment for recurrent UTI in kidney transplant patient
- Demonstrates feasibility of phage therapy in immunosuppressed renal patient

## Methods & Findings

Case report of 60-year-old kidney transplant recipient with recurrent UTIs treated with phage therapy. Relevant to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] management.
