# Goudman 2024 — Gut Dysbiosis in Chronic Pain (Meta-Analysis)

## Key Findings

- **Consistent alpha-diversity loss** in chronic pain conditions (Shannon index SMD = -0.27, p<0.001) across 21 studies.
- **Depleted taxa** in fibromyalgia: *Lachnospiraceae*, *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii*, *Roseburia* — all SCFA (butyrate) producers.
- **Enriched taxa:** *Eggerthella lenta*, *Flavonifractor plautii*.
- Pattern is shared across chronic low-back pain, fibromyalgia, and other chronic pain syndromes, suggesting a common gut–pain axis.

## Methods

Systematic review + meta-analysis of 21 studies (962 chronic pain patients, 1,212 controls). Alpha and beta diversity pooled analysis; taxa-level vote-counting and meta-analysis.
