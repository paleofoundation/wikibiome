# Altered Gut Mycobiome in ESRD Patients

## Key Claims

- ESRD patients had higher gut mycobiome diversity than healthy controls
- [[dysbiosis]] characterized by decrease in Saccharomyces cerevisiae and increase in opportunistic pathogens
- Aspergillus fumigatus, Exophiala spinifera enriched in ESRD
- Opportunistic pathogens correlated with creatinine and homocysteine levels

## Methods & Findings

223 ESRD patients and 69 healthy controls. Shotgun metagenomic sequencing with multi-omics analysis.
