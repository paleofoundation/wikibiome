# Metabolomic Approach to Identifying Platinum Resistance in Ovarian Cancer

## Key Claims

- 179/288 metabolites significantly different between platinum-sensitive and resistant OC cells
- Methionine degradation and cysteine biosynthesis were top canonical pathways affected
- Glutathione synthesis and polyamine biosynthesis most significantly altered in resistant cells
