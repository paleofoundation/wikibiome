# Gut Microbiome Remodeling in CKD

## Key Claims

- Hemodialysis associated with Firmicutes/Proteobacteria enrichment, reduced butyrate-producing taxa
- Peritoneal dialysis driven by glucose absorption and [[iron]] supplementation fosters pathogenic proliferation
- PBDs reduce inflammatory markers by 24% in PD patients
- FMT increases SCFA levels and lowers TMAO in diabetic nephropathy models

## Methods & Findings

Narrative review of kidney replacement therapy impacts on gut microbiota in [[chronic-kidney-disease]].
