# Estropausal Gut Microbiota Transplant Improves Ovarian Function

## Key Claims

- Heterochronic microbiota transfer remodels ovarian transcriptome and reduces inflammation-related gene expression
- FMT induces transcriptional features consistent with ovarian rejuvenation
- Enhanced ovarian health and increased fertility observed in recipient mice
- Direct causal link between gut microbiota and ovarian health demonstrated
