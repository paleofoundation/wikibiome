# Fungi and Tumors: The Role of Fungi in Tumorigenesis

## Key Claims

- Cancer type-specific fungal profiles exist across different tumor tissues
- Fungi influence tumorigenesis through host immunity, bioactive metabolites, microbiome interactions, and epigenetic factors
- FMT, probiotics, prebiotics may be therapeutic approaches targeting the fungal microbiome
