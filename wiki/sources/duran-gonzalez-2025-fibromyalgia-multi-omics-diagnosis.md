# Duran-Gonzalez 2025 — Multi-Omics Diagnosis of Fibromyalgia

## Key Findings

- **19 zOTUs** differentially abundant in FM including *Streptococcus salivarius*, *Mogibacterium*, *Collinsella*, and members of Actinomycetaceae.
- **30 plasma proteins** altered: zinc-alpha-2-glycoprotein (ZA2G) up 2.05-fold; glutathione peroxidase-3 (GPX3, selenium-dependent) down to 0.85-fold — both tie to metal-cofactor biology.
- **Multi-omics diagnostic panel** achieves AUC = 0.85, outperforming single-modality classifiers.
- Links FM to **zinc-handling** (ZA2G) and **selenium-dependent antioxidant capacity** (GPX3) — novel metallomic angle.

## Methods

Case-control (199 FM + 43 controls). 16S rRNA microbiome profiling + untargeted plasma proteomics. Feature selection + machine-learning classifier.
