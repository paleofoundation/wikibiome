# Probiotic Combination in CKD: Mouse Model and Clinical Pilot

## Key Claims

- Probiotics could attenuate renal fibrosis and improve renal function in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] mice
- Rate of eGFR decline significantly slower in probiotic group
- Serum TNF-alpha, IL-6, IL-18, and endotoxin significantly decreased after probiotic administration

## Methods & Findings

Mouse model (adenine-induced CKD) + clinical pilot (38 stage 3-5 CKD patients, 6-month probiotic intervention).
