# Burden of Diabetes Attributable to Dietary Cadmium Exposure

## Key Findings
- Dietary cadmium exposure contributes measurably to the global burden of type 2 diabetes
- Cadmium drives insulin resistance and beta-cell dysfunction through oxidative stress and mitochondrial damage
- Population-attributable fraction of T2D linked to Cd exposure is significant in high-exposure regions
