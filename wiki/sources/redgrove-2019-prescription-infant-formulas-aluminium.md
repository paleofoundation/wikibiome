## Key Claims

- All 24 prescription infant formulas tested were contaminated with [[aluminum]], with ready-to-drink formulas ranging from 49.9 to 1956.3 ug/L and powdered formulas from 0.27 to 3.27 ug/g
- Nutritional supplements for weight gain (ready-to-drink) were the most contaminated, with Abbott Nutrition PediaSure Plus Juice Apple at 1956.3 ug/L (391.3 ug per serving)
- The Danone Nutricia Fortini product range consistently showed high Al (500-800 ug/L)
- Some prescription formulas (e.g., SMA Nutrition Alfamino at 41.4 ug/L) had lower Al than any off-the-shelf formula previously measured, demonstrating contamination is not inevitable
- Powdered products were generally less contaminated than ready-to-drink products
- The allergy/intolerance formulas ranged 0.35-3.27 ug/g (powdered), with daily intake of 26-367 ug Al at 6 months
- The contamination of infant formulas by aluminum is not inevitable -- premium ingredients can reduce it
- Breast milk has aluminium content an order of magnitude lower than formula feeds
- There are no acceptable guidelines for human exposure to aluminum in adults, let alone newborn infants

## Methods/Findings

- **Design**: 24 prescription infant formulas obtained through Paediatric Clinic at Russells Hall Hospital, Dudley, UK; both ready-to-drink and powdered; unopened/pristine samples
- **Analysis**: Transversely heated graphite furnace atomic absorption spectrometry (TH GFAAS) after acid/peroxide microwave digestion; n=5 replicate measurements per product
- **Preterm/IUGR formulas (Table 1)**: Ready-to-drink Al 49.9-249.4 ug/L (3.5-45.7 ug per serving)
- **Weight gain supplements (Table 2)**: Ready-to-drink Al 153.5-1956.3 ug/L (25.6-391.3 ug per serving)
- **Allergy/intolerance powders (Table 3)**: 0.35-3.27 ug/g; daily Al intake ranged 26-367 ug at birth to 47-367 ug at 6 months; Nutramigen Pregestimil Lipil highest at 3.27 ug/g
- **Amino acid powders (Table 4)**: 0.27-2.23 ug/g; Nutramigen Puramino highest at 2.23 ug/g
- **Combined ranking (Table 5)**: When prepared per manufacturer instructions, Al ranged from 41.4 ug/L (SMA Alfamino) to 1956.3 ug/L (PediaSure Plus Juice Apple)
- **High %RSD** across most products suggests non-uniform aluminum distribution
