## Key Findings

- **Siderophore-antibiotic conjugates exploit bacterial iron transport** to bypass the outer membrane barrier that defeats conventional antibiotics in MDR Gram-negatives
- **BAL30072 (sulfactam siderophore)** shows synergy with carbapenems against MDR [[acinetobacter-baumannii]] and [[pseudomonas-aeruginosa]]
- **Active siderophore-mediated transport achieves MICs 100-fold lower** than passive diffusion, turning the pathogen's own iron hunger against it
