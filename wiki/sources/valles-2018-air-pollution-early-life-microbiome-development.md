# Key Claims

- **Altered microbiome in early life** impacts immune, metabolic, and neurological development
- **Reduced exposure to microbes, antibiotic use, poor dietary choices, and environmental pollution** challenge early microbial community establishment
- **Early alterations** in microbiota establishment can have long-term consequences for atopic disease, obesity, and autism
- **Dietary and medical strategies** to counteract early microbiota imbalances should inform public health policies
