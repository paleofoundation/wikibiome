# Mediterranean Diet and Ovarian Cancer Survival

## Key Claims

- High AMED intake was associated with improved OS in OC patients (HR=0.59, 95% CI 0.38-0.90)
- Decreased AMED intake from pre- to post-diagnosis linked to worse survival
- Maintaining consistently high AMED intake could benefit OC prognosis
