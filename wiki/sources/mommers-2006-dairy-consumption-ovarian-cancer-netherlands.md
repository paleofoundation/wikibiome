# Dairy Consumption and Ovarian Cancer Risk in the Netherlands Cohort Study

## Key Claims

- No association between consumption of milk, yoghurt, cheese, or fermented dairy products and OC risk
- Lactose or dairy fat intakes were not associated with serous ovarian cancer risk

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: Prospective cohort; 62,573 postmenopausal women, 11.3 years follow-up; 252 OC cases
