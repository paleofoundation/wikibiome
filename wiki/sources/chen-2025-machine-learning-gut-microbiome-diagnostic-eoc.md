# Machine Learning Diagnostic Model of EOC Based on Gut Microbiome Signatures

## Key Claims

- First comprehensive analysis of gut microbiota in EOC with differential profiles from benign OT and healthy controls
- Microbial-Cally diagnostic model achieved AUC of 0.976
- In vitro: Bifidobacterium possessed anti-tumor activity while E. coli exhibited pro-tumor activity
- Eight specific metabolites exclusively derived from the microbiome identified
