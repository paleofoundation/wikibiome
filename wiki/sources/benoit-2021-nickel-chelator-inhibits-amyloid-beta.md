## Key Findings

- **Nickel is the most abundant metal contaminant** in recombinant Abeta40 at 72.5 mmol/mol, and enhances aggregation 5.7-fold ([[benoit-2021-nickel-chelator-inhibits-amyloid-beta]], in-vitro).
- **Zinc drives the strongest aggregation** (14-fold), but nickel is a previously unrecognized contributor.
- **DMG (dimethylglyoxime)**, a nickel-specific chelator, inhibited Abeta40 aggregation by 40–85%, suggesting nickel removal as a therapeutic target.
