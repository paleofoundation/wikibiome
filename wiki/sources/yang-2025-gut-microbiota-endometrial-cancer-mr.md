# Causal Relationship Between Gut Microbiota and Endometrial Cancer

## Key Claims

- Family Acidaminococcaceae and genus Butyrivibrio identified as risk factors for general EC
- Family Lactobacillaceae and genus Coprococcus3 protective for non-endometrioid histology EC
- Specific gut microbiota associated with different pathological types of EC
