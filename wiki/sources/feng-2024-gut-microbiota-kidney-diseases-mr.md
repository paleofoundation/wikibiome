# Causal Relationship between Gut Microbiota and Kidney Diseases

## Key Claims

- Critical associations of 12 bacterial genera with nephrotic syndrome
- 7 bacterial genera with membranous nephropathy, 3 with glomerulonephritis
- 7 bacterial genera with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] identified
- Reverse MR did not reveal causal relationships, strengthening primary findings

## Methods & Findings

Two-sample MR using GWAS summary statistics. Multiple kidney disease subtypes analyzed including CKD, nephrotic syndrome, glomerulonephritis, and IgA nephropathy.
