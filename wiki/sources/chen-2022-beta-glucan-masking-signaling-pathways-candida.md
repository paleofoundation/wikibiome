# Key Findings

- **Lactate induces beta-glucan masking** through the Gpr1/cAMP-PKA signaling pathway, meaning lactate-rich environments (inflamed tissue, bacterial co-metabolism) actively help Candida evade immune detection ([[chen-2022-beta-glucan-masking-signaling-pathways-candida]], expert-opinion).
- **Iron limitation paradoxically induces masking** — the host's nutritional immunity response (Primitive 4) inadvertently triggers Candida's stealth mode (Primitive 6).
- **Hypoxia induces masking** through mitochondrial signaling pathways, linking Primitive 9 (oxygen state) to immune evasion.
