# Gut Mycobiome Dysbiosis and Hypertension

## Key Claims

- Pre-HTN and HTN groups had different fungal microbiome compared to normotension
- Malassezia relative abundance increased in HTN group
- Malassezia positively associated with serum immunoglobulin light chain concentrations
- Gut mycobiome may predict hypertension development in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] context

## Methods & Findings

Three cohorts (pre-HTN, HTN, normotension). ITS sequencing and immunoturbidimetric light chain assay.
