# Sami 2023 — Human Milk Nutrients Preventing NEC

## Key Findings

- **Zinc** is essential for intestinal barrier integrity, Paneth-cell defensin production, and preterm immunomodulation — a key mineral in the NEC-prevention arsenal.
- **Human milk oligosaccharides (HMOs)** are selectively metabolized by *Bifidobacterium* and *Lactobacillus*, shaping the preterm microbiome away from the Proteobacteria-dominated dysbiosis state.
- **L-tryptophan metabolism** yields indole-3-propionic acid (IPA), which activates AhR and PXR, reducing TNF-α and epithelial apoptosis.
- Selenium, calcium, and lactoferrin-iron handling complete the micronutrient layer of milk-mediated protection.

## Methods

Review of macronutrient, micronutrient, and bioactive components of human milk with proposed mechanisms of NEC protection.
