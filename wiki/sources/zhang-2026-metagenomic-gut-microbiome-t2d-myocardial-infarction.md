# Key Claims

- **DM+MI patients exhibit distinct gut microbial compositions** and functional gene signatures vs DM alone
- **Bacteroidales, Prevotellaceae, and Lachnospiraceae** enriched in DM+MI group
- **510 KEGG orthology units and 21 pathways** differed significantly including ABC transporters, quorum sensing, and general metabolic pathways
- **Random forest models** based on microbial species, KO units, and KEGG pathways achieved AUCs of 0.868, 0.885, and 0.820

# Methods & Findings

Shotgun metagenomic study: 29 DM+MI, 52 DM alone, quality-controlled to 29+33:

- Microbial diversity and metabolic profiles comparable overall between groups
- Significant differences in specific taxa and functional genes
- Carbohydrate transport/metabolism and glycoside hydrolase activity most enriched functional categories

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: Zinc deficiency is independently associated with both T2D and cardiovascular disease -- may be upstream driver of the distinct microbiome
- **[[iron]]**: Iron overload in cardiovascular disease patients may select for the pathogenic Bacteroidales enrichment observed
- **[[selenium]]**: Selenium-dependent glutathione peroxidase deficiency contributes to both cardiovascular and metabolic risk
- **[[gut-metal-microbiome]]**: Cardiovascular-diabetes comorbidity creates unique metal environment selecting distinct microbiome
- **[[dysbiosis]]**: Combined metabolic-cardiovascular [[dysbiosis]] may represent a distinct disease entity
