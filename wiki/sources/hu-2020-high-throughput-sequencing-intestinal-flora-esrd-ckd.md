# Intestinal Flora Changes in ESRD and CKD Patients

## Key Claims

- Decreased diversity and altered communities in peritoneal dialysis patients
- Microbial diversity positively correlated with albumin levels
- Butyrate-producing Faecalibacterium and Prevotellaceae dominant in healthy and [[chronic-kidney-disease]] non-dialysis groups
- Urease-containing and p-cresol-forming taxa dominant in PD group

## Methods & Findings

166 subjects: 17 healthy, 47 CKD non-dialysis, 49 HD, 53 PD. 16S rDNA sequencing.
