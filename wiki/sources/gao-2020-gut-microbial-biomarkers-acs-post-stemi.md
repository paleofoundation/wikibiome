# Gut Microbial Taxa as Potential Predictive Biomarkers for ACS and Post-STEMI Cardiovascular Events

## Summary

Prospective study investigating associations between gut microbial taxa, systemic [[tmao]] levels, and incident post-STEMI cardiovascular events. Enrolled 60 ACS patients (30 STEMI, 30 UAP) and 30 healthy controls from Tianjin Chest Hospital (2017). Combined 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing with TMAO and IL-6 measurement.

## Key Findings

### Study Design
- 90 consecutive cardiology patients after excluding 30
- ACS group: 30 STEMI + 30 UAP; Control group: 30 healthy volunteers
- Metagenomic sequencing and TMAO/IL-6 levels detected
- One-year follow-up for MACE in STEMI patients

### Microbial Composition in ACS
- At phylum level: ACS group dominant in *Elusimicrobia*; control group dominant in *Acidobacteria*
- ACS group enriched in *Coriobacteriaceae*, *Enterobacteriaceae*, *Moraxellaceae*, *Phylllobacteriaceae*; control group enriched in *Desulfurellaceae*
- At genus level: ACS enriched in *Escherichia-Shigella*, *Catenisphaera*, *Faecalitalea*, *Acinetobacter*, *Peptoclostridium*; controls enriched in *Lachnospira*
- 18 taxa of order/family/genera identified by LEfSe analysis (LDA effect score >3) as distinguishing ACS from controls

### TMAO Association
- Serum TMAO levels significantly increased in ACS patients vs. controls (P < 0.05)
- No significant differences between STEMI and UAP groups
- Five taxa whose abundance was significantly associated with serum TMAO: *Aerococcaceae*, *Ruminococcaceae_UCG.005*, *Ruminococcaceae_UCC.014*, *X. Eubacterium_fissicatena*, and decreased *Lachnospiraceae_FCS020* (P < 0.05)
- *Eubacterium_fissicatena* significantly increased in STEMI patients
- *Lachnospiraceae_FCS020* reduction associated with lower TMAO

### Post-STEMI MACE
- After 1-year follow-up, MACE occurred in 7/30 (23%) STEMI patients; 8/30 (27%) total events
- Kaplan-Meier analysis showed higher TMAO levels led to more efficient risk stratification
- Association between TMAO and MACE showed trend toward significance (P = 0.169)
- Gut microbial taxa including *Aerococcaceae*, *Eubacterium_fissicatena*, and *Lachnospiraceae_FCS020* not significantly associated with MACE occurrence
