# HRT Reverses Gut Microbiome and Serum Metabolome in POI

## Key Claims

- Eggerthella abundance significantly increased in POI patients and was reversed by HRT
- POI patients had altered serum metabolic signatures and increased TGF-beta1; reversed by HRT
- Estrogen ameliorated ovarian fibrosis induced by E. lenta in mice
