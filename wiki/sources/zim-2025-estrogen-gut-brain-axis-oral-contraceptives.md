# Estrogen-Gut-Brain Axis: COCs and Mental Health Through the Gut Microbiome

## Key Claims

- Combined oral contraceptives can alter gut microbiome composition and permeability
- The gut-estrogen axis examines how estrogens and the GI system interact to maintain the estrobolome
- COCs reduce diversity and bacterial richness in the gut
- Gut [[dysbiosis]] from COC use may contribute to anxiety and depression through the gut-brain axis

## Methods/Findings

- **Study type**: Review of animal and human studies on COC-gut microbiome-mental health interactions
