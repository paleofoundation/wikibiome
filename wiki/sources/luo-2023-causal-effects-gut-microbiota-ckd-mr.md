# Causal Effects of Gut Microbiota on the Risk of Chronic Kidney Disease

## Key Claims

- Desulfovibrionales causally associated with increased [[chronic-kidney-disease]] risk (OR = 1.15)
- Nine other taxa also detected with potential causalities with CKD
- Gut microbiota plays an important role in CKD pathogenesis
- New potential indicators and targets for CKD screening and prevention

## Methods & Findings

Two-sample MR with 196 gut bacterial taxa (N=18340) as instrumental variables. IVW, weighted median, MR-Egger, MR-PRESSO analyses.
