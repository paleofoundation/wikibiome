# Key Claims

- **Metformin administration in healthy volunteers** causes measurable gut microbiome [[dysbiosis]]
- **Drug-induced microbiome changes** occur independently of disease state
- **Metformin's GI side effects** may be mediated through microbiome disruption

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: Metformin may alter zinc-microbiome interactions even in healthy individuals
- **[[gut-metal-microbiome]]**: Drug-induced [[gut-metal-microbiome]] perturbation is an important confounder in diabetes studies
- **[[dysbiosis]]**: Distinguishing drug-induced from disease-driven [[dysbiosis]] requires careful study design
