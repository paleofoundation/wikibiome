# Kaelin 2022 — Gut Virome Signatures Preceding NEC

## Key Findings

- **Virome convergence** occurs approximately 10 days before clinical NEC onset — a novel predictive window independent of 16S bacterial signals.
- **137 viral contigs** were significantly associated with the 0–10 day pre-NEC period.
- NEC-associated **phages track their bacterial hosts** — enrichment correlates with Enterobacteriaceae, *Escherichia*, *Staphylococcus*, and *Proteus* expansion.
- Bifidobacterium-targeting phages are depleted, paralleling the bacterial depletion signal.

## Methods

Nested prospective case-control. 23 preterm infants (9 NEC, 14 controls). Viral metagenomic sequencing of serial stool samples. Contig assembly and host-prediction.
