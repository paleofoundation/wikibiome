# Gut Virome in PCOS Patients

## Key Claims

- PCOS patients showed decreased within-sample viral diversity and altered virome composition
- Siphoviridae depleted while Quimbyviridae enriched in PCOS
- Gut viral signatures achieved AUC of 0.938 for PCOS discrimination
