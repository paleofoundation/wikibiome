# Li 2026 — IBD and Male Erectile Dysfunction (Mechanistic Review)

## Key Findings

- Formalizes the **gut–penis axis**: gut-origin TNF-α and IL-6 systemically suppress endothelial NO synthase (eNOS), impairing NO-mediated corpus cavernosum relaxation.
- **Th17/Treg imbalance** driven by IBD propagates to the corpus cavernosum, sustaining local low-grade inflammation.
- **27% ED prevalence in men with IBD** — nearly double population baseline — supports the systemic-inflammation-to-vascular-dysfunction model.
- Integrates microbiome, immune, and vascular layers.

## Methods

Narrative review synthesizing IBD epidemiology, systemic inflammation biomarkers, and vascular physiology literature.
