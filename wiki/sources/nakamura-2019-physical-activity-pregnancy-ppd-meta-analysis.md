# Key Claims

- **Physical activity during pregnancy** is associated with reduced risk of [[postpartum-depression]]
- Systematic review and meta-analysis of studies examining exercise as a modifiable risk factor for PPD

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Systematic review and meta-analysis of observational and intervention studies
- **Setting**: INSERM, Sorbonne Universite, Paris, France and Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health

# Connections

- Exercise modulates [[gut-brain-axis]] function and gut microbiome composition
- Physical activity reduces [[inflammation]] and improves immune regulation
- Exercise-microbiome interactions relevant to [[dysbiosis]] prevention during pregnancy
- Lifestyle intervention that may complement nutritional strategies involving [[zinc]], [[iron]], [[magnesium]]
- Relevant to understanding modifiable risk factors for [[postpartum-depression]]
