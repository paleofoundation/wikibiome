# Metagenome-Wide Gut Microbial Signatures in ESRD

## Key Claims

- Fundamental alterations in ESRD microbiome: Blautia, Dorea, Eggerthellaceae elevated
- ESRD-depleted Prevotella, Roseburia species identified
- Gut microbial composition can predict serum uremic toxin concentrations in non-dialyzed [[chronic-kidney-disease]]

## Methods & Findings

Deep metagenomic sequencing of 378 hemodialytic ESRD patients and 290 healthy controls from two independent cohorts.
