# Causal Relationship Between Gut Microbiota and Gynecological Tumor

## Key Claims

- Higher relative abundance of Lachnospiraceae is associated with lower uterine fibroid risk (OR: 0.882)
- Higher OC incidence is associated with increased Lachnospiraceae abundance (OR: 1.329)
- Reverse MR showed no evidence of reverse causation
- Establishes a causal relationship between Lachnospiraceae and both UF and OC

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: Two-sample MR using MiBioGen GWAS and IEU Open GWAS
- **Methods**: IVW, WME, MR-Egger regression; sensitivity analyses
