# Key Claims

- **Human microbiome plays a pivotal role** in maintaining physiological balance throughout female reproductive health
- **Vaginal microbiome composition** changes throughout the menstrual cycle, with *Lactobacillus* more predominant during follicular phase
- **Dysbiosis in vaginal and gut microbiota** may have significant implications for fertility, pregnancy complications, and postpartum recovery including [[postpartum-depression]]
- Hormonal regulation, immune function, and [[inflammation]] are key microbiome-mediated pathways

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Narrative review covering microbiome involvement from menstruation through pregnancy and postpartum
- **Key topics**: Vaginal microbiome and hormonal influence, gut microbiome and reproductive health, therapeutic interventions
