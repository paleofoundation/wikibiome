# Metabolomics Profiling Distinguishes ESRD from CKD

## Key Claims

- 193 metabolites significantly altered in ESRD compared to [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Three kynurenine derivatives upregulated in ESRD
- Inhibiting kynurenine pathway may delay CKD-to-ESRD progression

## Methods & Findings

Chemical isotope labeling liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry on serum from 32 patients (13 ESRD, 19 CKD).
