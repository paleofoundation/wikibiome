# Dietary Composition Modulates Gut Microbiota in CKD

## Key Claims

- Distinct shift in gut microbiota in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients with significant reduction of SCFA-producing bacteria
- LP-HF diet displayed greater abundance of SCFA-producing taxa
- HP-LF diet showed enriched proteolytic genera (Klebsiella) with higher TMAO, IL-18, and MCP-1

## Methods & Findings

Cross-sectional study of 135 CKD patients and 19 healthy controls. 16S rRNA sequencing, TMAO, and cytokine analysis.
