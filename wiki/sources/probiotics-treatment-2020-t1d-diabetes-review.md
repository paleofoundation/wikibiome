# Key Claims

- **[[probiotics]] show therapeutic potential** for both T1D and T2D management through multiple mechanisms
- **Mechanisms include** restoration of gut barrier, immune modulation, SCFA production, and pathogen exclusion
- **Strain-specific effects** are important for clinical outcomes

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: Probiotic efficacy may be enhanced by zinc co-supplementation to support both probiotic survival and barrier function
- **[[iron]]**: [[probiotics]] compete with pathogens for iron, reducing pathogenic iron acquisition
- **[[selenium]]**: Selenium-enriched [[probiotics]] could provide dual metabolic-antioxidant benefit
- **[[gut-metal-microbiome]]**: [[probiotics]] reshape the [[gut-metal-microbiome]] toward protective configurations
- **[[intestinal-permeability]]**: Probiotic restoration of tight junctions requires adequate zinc and selenium
- **[[immune-balance]]**: Probiotic immune modulation operates through metal-dependent pathways
