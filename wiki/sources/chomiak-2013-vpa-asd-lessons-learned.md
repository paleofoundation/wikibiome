# Key Claims

- **Two recent epidemiological investigations** confirmed significant VPA-ASD risk in humans
- **Two important neurobiological findings** linking VPA to ASD: accelerated/early brain overgrowth and hyperexcitable networks
- **VPA has known teratogenicity** and has long been suspected as risk factor for ASD
- **VPA animal model** is one of the most widely used preclinical models of ASD
- Understanding how VPA alters early development of neurological systems can improve ASD understanding
