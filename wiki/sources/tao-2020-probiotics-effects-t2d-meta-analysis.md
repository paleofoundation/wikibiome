# Key Claims

- **Significantly greater reduction in HbA1c** (WMD = -0.24, 95% CI [-0.44, -0.04], p=0.02) in [[probiotics]]-treated group
- **FBG reduction** (WMD = -0.44 mmol/L, p=0.003) and **HOMA-IR reduction** (WMD = -1.07, p<0.00001) with [[probiotics]]
- **15 RCTs with 902 participants** included in the meta-analysis
- **Probiotics may reduce HbA1c, FBG, and insulin resistance** in T2DM patients

# Methods & Findings

Comprehensive meta-analysis of RCTs on probiotics in T2DM:

- Searched PubMed, Web of Science, Chinese databases before June 2019
- Random-effects model used for pooled WMD with 95% CI
- Sensitivity analysis verified reliability and stability of results
