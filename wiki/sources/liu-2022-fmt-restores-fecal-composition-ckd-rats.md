# FMT Restores Fecal Composition and Delays CKD in Rats

## Key Claims

- FMT successfully differentiates gut microbes and alters specific taxa within 1 week
- Bacteroides uniformis and Anaerostipes sp. 1XD22-93 increased in CKD/CKD group
- FMT considerably reduced protein-bound uremic toxins (PBUTs)
- Tryptophan metabolism suppressed and lysine biosynthesis promoted after FMT

## Methods & Findings

5/6 nephrectomy rat model. FMT from donor CKD and donor sham rats. Metagenome and metabolome analysis in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] context.
