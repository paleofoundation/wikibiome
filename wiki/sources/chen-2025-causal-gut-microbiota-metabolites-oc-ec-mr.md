# Causal Relationships of Gut Microbiota and Blood Metabolites with OC and EC

## Key Claims

- Six pathogenic bacterial taxa associated with OC including Euryarchaeota, Escherichia-Shigella, Prevotella9
- Christensenellaceae R7group and Tyzzerella3 were protective against OC
- 26 and 19 blood metabolites significantly correlated with OC and EC respectively
- FamilyXIIIAD3011group showed bidirectional causal relationship with OC
