# Blood Metabolites Mediate Gut Microbiota-Ovarian Cancer Relationships

## Key Claims

- 24 gut bacteria causally associated with OC: 10 risk factors and 14 protective factors
- Blood metabolites including lipoproteins, lipids, and amino acids mediated the gut microbiota-OC relationship
- Caffeic acid, caffeine metabolites, sphingomyelin, and ceramide metabolites were risk factors for OC

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: Two-sample MR with mediation analysis; MiBioGen (5,959 individuals); 233 metabolic traits; OCAC (63,702 participants)
