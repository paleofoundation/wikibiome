# Key Claims

- **ASD is highly heterogeneous** in presentation, comorbidities, and biological underpinnings
- **Gastrointestinal inflammation** is a significant association with ASD that varies across the spectrum
- **Exploring subgroups** within ASD based on GI inflammation status may reveal distinct biological mechanisms

# Methods & Findings

**Doctoral thesis** from UNSW Sydney examining autism heterogeneity and GI inflammation associations.

- Investigated the relationship between ASD presentation and GI inflammatory markers
- Explored how heterogeneity in ASD maps to GI comorbidity patterns
- Available as open-access resource from UNSW institutional repository
