## Key Findings

- **Multi-omics integration** (16S rRNA + metabolomics) revealed gut-brain metabolic crosstalk in CPE children.
- **[[bacteroides-fragilis]]** and Dialister were depleted in CPE, while [[phascolarctobacterium]] showed altered abundance.
- **Kynurenic acid, 2-oxindole, and dopamine were elevated** in CPE, implicating the kynurenine pathway in epileptogenesis through the gut-microbiota-brain axis (GMBA).
- The kynurenine pathway links tryptophan metabolism to neuroactive metabolites — microbial dysbiosis may shift this pathway toward excitotoxic or seizure-promoting metabolites.
- [[eubacterium]] abundance correlated with specific metabolic signatures.
