# MR Study Revealed a Gut Microbiota-Immune System-Kidney Junction Axis in CKD

## Key Claims

- Causal relationships between 8 gut microbiotas in MiBioGen and 8 in DMP with [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- 25 cytokine and immune cell characteristics associated with CKD
- Immune cells mediate effects of microbiome on CKD via CD28+ CD8RA+ CD8+ T cells
- Alistipes indistinctus and Alistipes putredinis affect CKD via immune cell activity

## Methods & Findings

Two-sample MR using CKDGen consortium, MiBioGen, DMP microbiome data, and immune cell traits from GWAS.
