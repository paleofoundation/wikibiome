# Key Claims

- **6-week antidepressant treatment** led to decreased Firmicutes/Bacteroidetes ratio due to enhanced Bacteroidetes and reduced Firmicutes
- **Fluoxetine was more potent than amitriptyline** at altering Firmicutes and Bacteroidetes levels
- **Both antidepressants** significantly increased abundance of Porphyromonadaceae
- **Increased Parabacteroides, Butyricimonas, and Alistipes** observed after both antidepressant treatments
- **Both antidepressants affected antibiotic resistome** including aminoglycoside, multidrug, and tetracycline resistance
