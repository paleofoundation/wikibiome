# Dietary Fiber Supplementation and Uremic Toxins in CKD

## Key Claims

- Dietary fiber significantly reduces indoxyl sulfate (SMD = -0.55) and p-cresyl sulfate (SMD = -0.47) in [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Significant reduction of blood urea nitrogen and uric acid with fiber supplementation
- No significant effect on creatinine reduction
- Reduction of indoxyl sulfate more pronounced in dialysis patients

## Methods & Findings

Meta-analysis of 10 RCTs involving 292 CKD patients. Pooled SMD with 95% CI.
