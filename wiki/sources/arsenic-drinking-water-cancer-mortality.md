# Arsenic in Drinking Water and Mortality for Cancer and Chronic Diseases

## Key Findings
- Arsenic in drinking water increases mortality from lung and bladder cancer in a dose-dependent manner
- Non-cancer mortality is also elevated, including cardiovascular disease and diabetes-related deaths
- Current regulatory safe limits for arsenic in drinking water may be insufficiently protective
