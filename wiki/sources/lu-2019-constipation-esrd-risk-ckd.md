# Constipation and ESRD Risk in CKD Patients

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients with de novo constipation have increased risk of ESRD (adjusted HR 1.90)
- More severe constipation associated with higher ESRD risk (HR up to 4.41)
- Gut microbiota disturbance through constipation may worsen CKD progression

## Methods & Findings

Nationwide database study (Taiwan) of newly diagnosed CKD patients (2000-2011). Propensity-matched Cox models.
