# Key Claims

- **7 metabolites** causally linked to [[postpartum-depression]] via bi-directional MR analysis of 1,400 metabolic biomarkers
- **1 metabolite protective**, 6 associated with increased PPD risk
- Results consistent across multiple MR methods, indicating significant causal correlation
- PPD sample: 67,205 mothers with complete postpartum follow-up (7,604 cases, 59,601 controls) from FinnGen

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Bi-directional two-sample MR; 1,091 blood-derived metabolites, 309 metabolite ratios; PPD SNPs from FinnGen; IVW, weighted median, model-based estimation
- **Setting**: Jiaxing Women and Children's Hospital, China
- **Key implication**: Metabolomic profiles offer new insights into PPD pathophysiology and targeted treatment strategies
