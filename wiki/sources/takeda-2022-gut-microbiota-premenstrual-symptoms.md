# Takeda 2022 — Gut Microbiota and Premenstrual Symptoms

## Key Findings

- **Bacteroidetes phylum** significantly reduced in women with premenstrual disorders (P = 0.015).
- **Parabacteroides and Megasphaera** negatively predicted premenstrual symptom questionnaire (PSQ) severity (R² = 0.29) — candidate protective genera.
- **Anaerotaenia** enriched and positively correlated with severity (r = 0.292).
- Early evidence that **estrobolome composition** (primitive 7) varies with premenstrual symptom burden.

## Methods

Cross-sectional case-control. 21 women with premenstrual disorders + 22 controls. 16S rRNA sequencing. Linear regression of taxa abundance on PSQ scores.
