# Polyphenol Intake and CKD Incidence

## Key Claims

- Moderate intake of lignans (6.8 mg or less) negatively associated with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] incidence (HR 0.97)
- No significant associations between higher amounts of lignans/total polyphenols and CKD
- Moderate lignin intake possesses CKD-protective properties by approximately 32%

## Methods & Findings

Prospective cohort of 3021 Iranian adults over 11,058 person-years. Food frequency questionnaire for polyphenol intake.
