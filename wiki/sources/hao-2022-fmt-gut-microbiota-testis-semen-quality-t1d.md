# Key Claims

- **Alginate oligosaccharide (AOS)-improved gut microbiota FMT (A10-FMT)** significantly decreased blood glucose and glycogen in STZ-induced T1D mice
- **A10-FMT improved T1D-disturbed gut microbiota**, especially increasing small intestinal Lactobacillus
- **Blood and testicular metabolome** shifted to produce n-3 PUFA (DHA and EPA) for spermatogenesis amelioration
- **Gut microbiota-testis axis** represents a novel therapeutic approach for T1D-related male infertility
- **AOS-improved gut microbiota** can improve spleen and liver functions for systemic benefit

# Methods & Findings

STZ-induced T1D mouse model (ICR/CD1 mice):

- FMT from AOS-improved gut microbiota vs conventional FMT tested
- Semen quality, gut microbiota, blood metabolism, liver, and spleen tissues assessed
- A10-FMT superior to conventional FMT for all outcomes
