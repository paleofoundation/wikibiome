# Etyemez 2025 — Biomarkers in Reproductive Psychiatric Disorders

## Key Findings

- **PMDD-specific cytokine signature:** IL-8 (CXCL-8) and TNF-α are elevated during the luteal phase — a measurable inflammatory signal tied to symptom cycling.
- **IL-8 positively correlates with symptom severity** — candidate biomarker for severity staging and treatment response.
- **Allopregnanolone (ALLO)** levels and **ALLO/progesterone ratios** distinguish PMDD from PMS and controls — neurosteroid axis biomarker.
- Integrates inflammation, neurosteroid, and gonadal-hormone layers.

## Methods

Review synthesizing biomarker literature on PMDD, postpartum depression, and perimenopausal depression.
