# The Gut Microbiome, Diet, and CKD

## Key Claims

- Disruption of gut microbiota is associated with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] and its complications
- Diet is one of the most important factors influencing gut microbiota diversity
- Mediterranean diet with probiotic/prebiotic or synbiotic direction ideal for CKD patients

## Methods & Findings

Review of gut microbiota composition, dietary factors, and CKD relationship.
