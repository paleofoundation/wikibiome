# Dietary Fibre Intake and Risk of Incident CKD

## Key Claims

- Inverse association between total fibre intake and [[chronic-kidney-disease]] risk (OR 0.47 for highest quartile)
- Every 5 g/d increase in total fibre reduces CKD risk by 11%
- Fiber from vegetables and legumes most strongly protective

## Methods & Findings

Population-based prospective study of 1630 Tehran Lipid and Glucose Study participants over 6.1 years.
