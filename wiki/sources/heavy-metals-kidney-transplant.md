# Effects of Heavy Metal Exposure on Kidney Transplant Recipients

## Key Findings
- Heavy metal body burden is associated with increased risk of transplant rejection in kidney recipients
- Cadmium, mercury, and lead accumulate in renal tissue and impair graft function through nephrotoxic mechanisms
- Pre-transplant metal screening and post-transplant exposure reduction may improve graft survival
