## Key Findings

- **Metallophores as diagnostic biomarkers**: Microbial metallophores detected by mass spectrometry can distinguish invasive infection from colonization — a clinical game-changer ([[patil-2021-infection-metallomics-covid-era]], expert-opinion).
- **Nutritional immunity drives virulence**: Host metal sequestration forces pathogens into secondary metabolism, producing detectable metallophores.
- **Gallium as therapeutic decoy**: Gallium mimics Fe³⁺ and disrupts iron-dependent pathogen metabolism.
