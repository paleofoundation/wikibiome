# The Gut Microbiome Tango in CKD Progression

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] disturbs gut microbiome resulting in imbalanced composition and metabolite changes
- Dysbiotic gut microbiota negatively impacts immune system and aggravates disease
- Effects of uremic toxins on immune system reviewed
- Current sequencing technologies and novel therapies targeting gut microbiota discussed

## Methods & Findings

Review of gut microbiota roles in CKD progression with focus on immune modulation.
