# Key Claims

- **Neuroinflammation** is closely linked to depressive pathogenesis; MDD may be called a "microglia disease"
- **P2X7R/NLRP3 signaling pathway** in microglia is a key mechanism causing nerve damage in depression
- **Gut microbiota can initiate neuroinflammatory processes** underlying MDD; [[dysbiosis]] can be affected by sleep
- *Bifidobacterium adolescentis* probiotic has demonstrated therapeutic effects by reducing hippocampal pro-inflammatory cytokines (IL-1B, TNF-alpha)
- **Chronic sleep disruption** impacts gut microbiota and induces adipose tissue [[inflammation]] and insulin resistance

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Review of gut-brain axis interactions with neuroinflammation in MDD
- **Key mechanisms**: Barrier dysfunction, LPS translocation, oxidative stress cascades, GABA/serotonin production by *Bifidobacterium* and *Lactobacillus*
