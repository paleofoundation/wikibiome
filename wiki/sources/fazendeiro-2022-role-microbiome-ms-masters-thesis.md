# Role of Microbiome in Multiple Sclerosis

## Summary

This master's thesis from the University of Lisbon (Faculty of Pharmacy) provides a comprehensive review of the role of the [[gut-microbiome]] in [[multiple-sclerosis]], covering the gut-brain axis, dysbiosis in MS, microbial metabolites, and therapeutic implications including [[probiotics]] and [[fecal-microbiota-transplant]].

## Relevance to Gut-Metal-Microbiome Axis

As a comprehensive educational review, this thesis synthesizes the current understanding of how [[gut-microbiome]] composition relates to MS pathogenesis. While not presenting novel data, it provides a structured overview relevant to the broader [[gut-brain-axis]] framework.

## Limitations

- Master's thesis, not peer-reviewed
- Review of existing literature without novel experimental data
- Limited to information available up to 2022
