# The Role of Bile Acids in Carcinogenesis

## Key Claims

- Bile acids at supraphysiological concentrations are pro-carcinogenic, but at reference concentrations some possess anticancer activity
- UDCA has almost purely anticancer properties and is a candidate for drug repurposing
- Differences in cancer responses to bile acids lie in differential expression of bile acid receptors (FXR vs. TGR5)
- The gut microbiome converts primary to secondary bile acids, modulating cancer risk across multiple cancer types including ovarian

## Methods/Findings

- **Study type**: Comprehensive review covering bile acid involvement in 8+ cancer types
- **Key receptors**: FXR, TGR5, PXR, CAR, VDR, LXR, muscarinic receptors CHRM2/3
