# Shtrozberg 2025 — Gut Microbiome in Fibromyalgia (Review)

## Key Findings

- **Causal evidence from FMT:** transfer of stool from FM patients to germ-free mice induced **mechanical pain hypersensitivity** — strong support for causal direction.
- **Micronutrient layer:** magnesium blocks NMDA receptors (anti-excitotoxic); selenium protects against oxidative neural damage; **low ferritin (<50 ng/mL) carries a 6.5-fold increased FM risk**.
- Converging taxa signal: depleted *Faecalibacterium* and *Bifidobacterium*; enriched *Flavonifractor* and *Eisenbergiella*.
- Integrates primitive 2 (nutritional immunity — low iron may be functional defense or true deficit) with primitive 5 (ecological engineering).

Narrative and critical review covering microbiome, FMT evidence, and micronutrient contributions to FM pathophysiology.
