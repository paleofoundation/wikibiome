# Metformin Modulates Gut Microbiome in Broiler Breeder Hens

## Key Claims

- Metformin impacted microbial community composition at 75 mg/kg dose-dependently
- Ruminococcus and Clostridia family differentially abundant
- Broiler breeder hens serve as PCOS model due to similar phenotype
