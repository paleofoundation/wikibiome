# Effects of Dietary Fat on Gut Microbiome and Ovarian Cancer in Mouse Model

## Key Claims

- KD and HF/LC diets significantly accelerated EOC tumor growth compared to LF/HC diet
- Gut microbiome diversity markedly reduced in KD and HF/LC-fed mice, correlating with tumor growth
- LF/HC-fed mice showed higher diversity and distinct taxa
- Functional pathways in KD mice indicated polyamine biosynthesis and fatty acid oxidation
