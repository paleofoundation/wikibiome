# Manganese in Salmonella Pathogenesis: Beyond Antioxidant Defense

## Key Findings

- **Mn is more than SOD**: Manganese supports *Salmonella* virulence beyond the canonical MnSOD antioxidant defense — Mn-dependent enzymes are essential for **carbon metabolism and metabolic homeostasis** under nitrosative stress inside macrophages [[upadhyay-2022-manganese-salmonella-beyond-antioxidant]], expert-opinion.
- **Expanded Achilles' heel**: Host [[calprotectin]]-mediated Mn sequestration doesn't just disable the antioxidant defense — it cripples entire metabolic pathways, making Mn restriction even more potent than previously understood.
