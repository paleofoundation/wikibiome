# Key Claims

- **Significant difference in beta diversity** but not alpha diversity between GDM and normal glucose tolerance groups
- **Ruminococcus bromii, Clostridium colinum, and Streptococcus infantis** higher in GDM group
- **S. infantis correlated positively** with blood glucose levels (strong positive correlation after BMI adjustment)
- **S. infantis may confer increased GDM risk** and the gut microbiome may serve as potential therapeutic target

# Methods & Findings

Prospective study in 33 pregnant Chinese women (15 GDM, 18 NGT) at 24-28 weeks:

- 16S rRNA gene amplicon sequencing performed
- Three abnormally expressed intestinal bacteria identified in GDM
- Quantitative PCR confirmed putative bacterial markers
