# Microbiota-Derived Metabolites in Ascites of Ovarian and GI Cancer

## Key Claims

- OC stage IV exhibited metabolic profiles similar to GI cancers; stage II-III differed significantly
- Stage IV OC had higher levels of 11 typically microbiome-derived metabolites
- Metabolites like 3-hydroxyanthranilic acid, indole, and naringenin may serve as markers of disease progression
