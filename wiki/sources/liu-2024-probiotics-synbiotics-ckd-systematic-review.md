# Probiotics/Synbiotics Supplementation in CKD: Systematic Review

## Key Claims

- Probiotics/synbiotics significantly reduced BUN (SMD -0.23) and CRP (SMD -0.34) in [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Longer-term supplementation more favorable for CKD patients
- 21 RCTs included; effective for improving renal function and inflammation indices

## Methods & Findings

Systematic review and meta-analysis of 21 RCTs.
