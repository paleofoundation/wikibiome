# Key Claims

- **Lactobacillus casei 431** supplementation (500mg capsules, 1.5x10^9 CFU/ml daily) from day 1 to day 21 postpartum showed **no significant effect** on PPD symptoms vs. placebo
- Mean depression scores at day 5 were 4.7 (probiotic) vs. 5.6 (placebo); at day 21 were 7.39 vs. 8.11 (not significant, p=0.71)
- **PPD prevalence in Iranian women**: 9.8-25.7%
- Authors suggest starting probiotic supplementation during pregnancy (longer duration) may yield different results

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Double-blind, placebo-controlled RCT; 74 postpartum women; Al-Zahra Hospital, Tabriz, Iran
- **Assessment**: Depression scores at 5, 15, and 21 days postpartum using Edinburgh Depression Scale
- **Key limitation**: Short intervention duration (21 days) may have been insufficient for microbiome modulation
