# Tryptophan and Indole Metabolites in Follicular Fluid with Diminished Ovarian Reserve

## Key Claims

- TRP, IPA, and IAA levels in follicular fluid significantly lower in DOR vs. NOR groups (P<0.01)
- TRP in FF positively correlated with available embryo rate in NOR females
- Indole metabolites from intestinal flora may serve as biomarkers and ovarian protectors
