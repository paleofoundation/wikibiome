# Disruption of Zinc Homeostasis Reverses Tigecycline Resistance in Klebsiella pneumoniae

## Key Findings

- **Zinc ionophores reverse AMR**: Elevating intracellular zinc via ionophores suppresses **superoxide dismutase (SOD)** and increases [[reactive-oxygen-species|ROS]], overcoming tigecycline resistance in *[[klebsiella-pneumoniae]]* [[wang-2025-zinc-homeostasis-reverses-tigecycline-resistance-klebsiella]], in-vitro.
- **Metal intoxication as antimicrobial strategy**: This is the opposite of DMG nickel chelation — instead of starving pathogens of essential metals, this approach **poisons them with excess metal**. Both strategies exploit metal homeostasis as an Achilles' heel (Primitive 4).
- **Drug resensitization**: Zinc ionophores could rescue existing antibiotics that have lost efficacy, avoiding the need for entirely new antibiotic classes.
