# Key Claims

- **Oral glutathione treatment** showed significant increases in plasma reduced glutathione but not whole-blood glutathione
- **Both oral and transdermal treatment groups** showed significant increases in plasma sulfate, cysteine, and taurine
- **Glutathione supplementation** may have benefit in improving transsulfuration metabolites in ASD
- **Open-label trial** with oral lipoceutical glutathione (n=13) or transdermal glutathione (n=13), ages 3-13
- ASD children have **significantly lower glutathione levels** than typically developing children
