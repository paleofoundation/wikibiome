# Risk and Prognosis of Ovarian Cancer in Women with Endometriosis

## Key Claims

- Endometriosis increased OC risk (RR 1.265, 95% CI 1.214-1.318)
- EAOC showed favourable characteristics including early-stage disease and specific histology
