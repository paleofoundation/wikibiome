# Chiral Amino Acid Metabolomics for CKD Biomarker Screening

## Key Claims

- D-Ser, D-Pro, and D-Asn strongly associated with kidney function (eGFR)
- D-Ser and D-Asn significantly associated with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] progression
- Risk of ESKD or death elevated 2.7-3.8 fold with higher D-Ser and D-Asn levels
- Chiral amino acids identified as potential biomarkers for CKD prognosis

## Methods & Findings

Enantioselective analysis of all chiral amino acids via 2D-HPLC in plasma from 108 CKD patients in longitudinal cohort.
