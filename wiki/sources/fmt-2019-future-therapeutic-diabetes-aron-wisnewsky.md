# Key Claims

- **FMT represents a future therapeutic option** for diabetes management through gut microbiome restoration
- **Evidence from metabolic syndrome studies** supports FMT efficacy for insulin sensitivity improvement
- **Donor selection and engraftment factors** are critical determinants of FMT success
