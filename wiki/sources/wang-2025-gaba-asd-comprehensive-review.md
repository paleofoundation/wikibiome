# Key Claims

- **GABA** is closely related to ASD pathogenesis as an inhibitory neurotransmitter
- **Abnormalities in GABAergic neurons**, signaling pathways, and related genes play important roles in ASD
- **Abnormal GABA levels** found in blood and brain tissue of ASD individuals
- **GABAergic drugs** have shown potential to improve ASD symptoms in animal models
- Prevalence of ASD in the US is now 27.6 per 1,000 (1 in every 36 children)
