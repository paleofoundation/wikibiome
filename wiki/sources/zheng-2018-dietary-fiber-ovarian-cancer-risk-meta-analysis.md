# Dietary Fiber Intake and Reduced Risk of Ovarian Cancer

## Key Claims

- Significant inverse dose-response association between dietary fiber intake and OC risk
- Pooled RR for highest vs. lowest category was 0.78 (95% CI: 0.70-0.88) with no heterogeneity
- Each 10 g/day increase in fiber associated with 12% reduced OC risk (RR: 0.88)

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: Meta-analysis of 13 studies (5,777 OC cases, 142,189 participants)
