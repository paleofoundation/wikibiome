# Bile Acids in Follicular Fluid: Therapeutic Targets for Diminished Ovarian Reserve

## Key Claims

- Levels of lithocholic, chenodeoxycholic, ursodeoxycholic, deoxycholic, and cholic acid in FF were significantly lower in DOR patients
- A diagnostic model based on five bile acids achieved AUC of 0.964
- BA levels in FF are closely related to evaluation of ovarian reserve function

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: 182 women undergoing ART; 91 NOR, 91 DOR; targeted metabolomics
- **Methods**: Random forest diagnostic model; transcriptome sequencing of granulosa cells
