# Bacterial Classification in PD Effluent of CKD Patients

## Key Claims

- Five major bacteria species identified in PD effluent via 16S rDNA metagenomic approach
- Metagenomic approach has greater potential than traditional culture for bacterial identification

## Methods & Findings

Nanopore sequencing-based metagenomic analysis of PD effluent from [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients.
