# The Gut Microbiome and Ovarian Cysts: MR Study

## Key Claims

- 17 gut microbial genera with suggestive causal associations with ovarian cysts
- Nine potential risk factors and eight protective genera identified
- Estrogen, androgen, and insulin interactions between gut microbiome and ovarian cysts are key pathways
