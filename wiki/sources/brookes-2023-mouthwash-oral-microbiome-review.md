# Key Findings

- **Antimicrobial mouthwashes cause oral dysbiosis**: CHX, CPC, and essential oil-based mouthwashes non-selectively kill oral species, leaving resistant organisms to dominate [[brookes-2023-mouthwash-oral-microbiome-review]], expert-opinion.
- **Systemic consequences**: Oral dysbiosis linked to cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and potentially cancer through oral-systemic translocation pathways [[brookes-2023-mouthwash-oral-microbiome-review]].
- The review highlights the tension between infection control and microbiome preservation in oral care.

## Relevance to WikiBiome

- Connects to the oral-breast cancer axis via the mechanism of mouthwash-induced dysbiosis → altered oral-systemic translocation.
- [[porphyromonas-gingivalis]] — CHX use affects P. gingivalis abundance; paradoxically may select for resistant strains.
