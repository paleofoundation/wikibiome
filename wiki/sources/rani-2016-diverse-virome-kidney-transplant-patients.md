# Diverse Virome in Kidney Transplant Patients

## Key Claims

- Complex urinary virome in kidney transplant patients with multiple viral subtypes
- BK virus predominant in BKV+ group, also found in BKV- group
- JC virus and Torque teno virus also detected

## Methods & Findings

Metagenomic shotgun sequencing of urine samples from kidney transplant recipients. Relevant to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] complications.
