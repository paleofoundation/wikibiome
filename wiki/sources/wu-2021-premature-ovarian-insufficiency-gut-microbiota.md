# Association Between Premature Ovarian Insufficiency and Gut Microbiota

## Key Claims

- POI women had altered microbial profiles: reduced Bulleidia and Faecalibacterium; enriched Butyricimonas, Dorea, Lachnobacterium, Sutterella
- Alterations closely related to FSH, LH, E2, AMH, and FSH/LH ratio
