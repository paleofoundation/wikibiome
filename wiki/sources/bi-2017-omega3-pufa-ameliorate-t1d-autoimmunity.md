# Key Claims

- **Omega-3 PUFAs sharply reduced T1D incidence** in NOD mice by modulating Th cell differentiation and decreasing Tregs
- **Decreased levels of IFN-gamma, IL-17, IL-6** and decreased Th1/Th17 differentiation with omega-3
- **Omega-3 PUFA eicosanoid derivatives** and mTOR complex 1 (mTORC1) inhibition mediated CD4+ T cell regulation
- **Therapeutic intervention in NOD mice** via nutritional supplementation or lentivirus-mediated omega-3 fatty acid desaturase expression normalized glucose and insulin for 182+ days
- **Blocked development of autoimmunity**, prevented lymphocyte infiltration into regenerated islets

# Methods & Findings

Preclinical study in NOD mouse model of T1D:

- Dietary omega-3 supplementation and genetic approaches tested
- Expression of beta-cell markers Pdx1 and Pax4 elevated
- mTORC1 signaling pathway identified as key mechanism
- Both preventive and therapeutic effects demonstrated
