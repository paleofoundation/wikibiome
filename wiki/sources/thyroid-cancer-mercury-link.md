# Elucidating the Link Between Thyroid Cancer and Mercury Exposure

## Key Findings
- Mercury exposure shows a dose-response relationship with thyroid cancer incidence
- Mercury accumulates preferentially in thyroid tissue due to the gland's high metabolic activity and iodine transport mechanisms
- Dietary mercury from seafood is the primary exposure route in the Northern Italian cohort
