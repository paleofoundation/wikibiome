# Key Claims

- **Quantitative metabolomics confirms altered metabolic pathways** in diabetes mellitus
- **1H-NMR and LC-MS/MS platforms** provide complementary metabolomic coverage for diabetes characterization
- **Multiple metabolic pathways disrupted** in diabetes including amino acid, lipid, and carbohydrate metabolism

# Methods & Findings

Quantitative metabolomics study using dual analytical platforms:

- 1H-NMR spectroscopy for broad metabolite profiling
- LC-MS/MS for targeted quantification of metabolites
- Altered pathways identified across multiple metabolic domains

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: Many altered metabolic pathways in diabetes involve zinc-dependent enzymes
- **[[iron]]**: Iron-dependent metabolic pathways are central to the alterations observed
- **[[selenium]]**: Selenoprotein-dependent pathways intersect with diabetes-altered metabolism
- **[[gut-metal-microbiome]]**: Metabolomic profiles in diabetes reflect both host and microbial metal-dependent metabolism
