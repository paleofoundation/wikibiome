# Protein-Bound Uremic Toxins and Inflammatory Markers in CKD

## Key Claims

- Plasma uremic toxins (IS, p-CS, IAA) positively correlated with IL-6 levels
- IS was an independent predictor for IL-6 and MCP-1 levels
- HD patients had higher inflammatory markers and uremic toxins than non-dialysis patients
- Plasma uremic toxins associated with higher IL-6 and MCP-1 in [[chronic-kidney-disease]]

## Methods & Findings

Cross-sectional study: 29 non-dialysis CKD + 29 hemodialysis patients. HPLC for uremic toxins, immunoassays for cytokines and [[calprotectin]].
