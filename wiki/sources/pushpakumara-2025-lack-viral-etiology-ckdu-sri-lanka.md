# No Evidence for Viral Etiology in CKDu in Sri Lanka

## Key Claims

- No evidence for viral etiology in symptomatic CKDu in Sri Lanka
- Serum screening against known pathogenic viruses and kidney biopsy NGS negative
- Viral infections (Hantavirus, CMV, EBV) unlikely causative agents in CKDu

## Methods & Findings

Prospective observational cohort of 151 symptomatic patients in CKDu-endemic Sri Lanka. VirScan and NGS analysis. Relevant to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] of unknown etiology.
