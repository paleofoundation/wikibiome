# Intestinal Fungi and Heart Failure Progression in CKD

## Key Claims

- Ascomycota and Candiada-Lodderomyces-clade fungi significantly enriched in [[chronic-kidney-disease]]+HF patients
- CKD mice with dual fungal + toxin treatment showed decreased LVEF and LVFS
- Intestinal fungi associated with CKD-HF progression
- Gut fungi may become novel therapeutic target for CKD-HF

## Methods & Findings

Clinical study (CKD, CKD+HF, healthy controls; 18S rRNA sequencing) plus C57BL/6J mouse model.
