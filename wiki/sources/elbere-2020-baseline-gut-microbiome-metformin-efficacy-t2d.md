# Key Claims

- **Metformin reduced alpha diversity** in healthy controls (p=0.02) but not in T2D patients
- **Species-level reductions** in Clostridium bartlettii and Barnesiella intestinihominis in both cohorts
- **Increase in Parabacteroides distasonis and Oscillibacter** in both study groups
- **Increased Prevotella copri** in Non-Responders subgroup (FDR=0.01)
- **Baseline microbiome predicts therapeutic response** -- Enterococcus faecium, Lactococcus lactis, Odoribacter, and Dialister enriched at baseline in Responders

# Methods & Findings

Longitudinal observational study: 35 healthy + 50 newly diagnosed T2D Latvian population:

- Shotgun metagenomics at multiple time points during metformin administration
- Responders vs Non-Responders defined by HbA1c decrease threshold
- Taxonomic and functional changes correlated with therapeutic outcomes
