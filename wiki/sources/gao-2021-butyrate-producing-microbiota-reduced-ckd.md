# Butyrate Producing Microbiota Are Reduced in CKD

## Key Claims

- 19 microbial genera significantly associated with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] severity
- Butyrate-producing bacteria reduced in advanced CKD stages
- Functional metagenomics showed two-component systems and metabolic activity associated with severity

## Methods & Findings

Shot-gun metagenomics sequencing of 52 CKD patients across stages 3A-5 (ESKD).
