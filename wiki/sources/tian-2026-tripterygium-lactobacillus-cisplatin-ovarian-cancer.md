# Tripterygium Glycosides and Lactobacillus paracasei Sensitize Ovarian Cancer to Cisplatin

## Key Claims

- TG significantly enhanced cisplatin sensitivity in EOC, either alone or synergistically with FMT
- TG inhibited the Keap1-Nrf2-GPX4 axis, inducing tumor [[ferroptosis]]
- Gut microbiota mediated TG's chemosensitizing effects; antibiotic-depleted mice showed reduced benefit
- L. paracasei co-administration with TG produced synergistic effects

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: In vivo mouse models with FMT from healthy controls
- **Mechanistic focus**: Keap1-Nrf2-GPX4 signaling pathway and [[ferroptosis]] induction
