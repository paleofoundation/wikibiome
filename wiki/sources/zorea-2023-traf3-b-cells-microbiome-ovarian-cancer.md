# TRAF3 Suppression Encourages B Cell Recruitment in Microbiome-Intact OC Mice

## Key Claims

- TRAF3 is a tumor-derived immune-suppressive modulator in OC
- TRAF3 knockout activated type-I interferon pathway and increased MHC-I expression
- Commensal microbiota necessary for B-cell-mediated antitumor immunity
- Unique IgA-coated bacteria profiles found in ascites of OC-bearing mice
