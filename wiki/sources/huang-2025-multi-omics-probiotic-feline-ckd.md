# Multi-Omics Analysis of Probiotic Strains in Feline CKD

## Key Claims

- Lactobacillus intervention modulated gut microbial composition and metabolic functions in feline [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Creatinine and BUN levels stabilized or reduced in most cats
- High responders showed modulation of pathways involved in GDUTs, tryptophan, phenylalanine, and SCFAs

## Methods & Findings

8-week Lactobacillus intervention in cats with stage 2-3 CKD. Full-length 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing and untargeted metabolomics.
