# Gut Microbiota and the Gut-Brain-Kidney Axis in CKD

## Key Claims

- Gut [[dysbiosis]] contributes to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] progression via systemic inflammation and metabolic imbalances
- Uremic toxins (indoxyl sulfate, p-cresyl sulfate) impair kidney and neurocognitive function
- Bidirectional signaling within gut-brain-kidney axis opens avenues for novel biomarkers

Narrative review integrating gut-brain-kidney axis research in CKD.
