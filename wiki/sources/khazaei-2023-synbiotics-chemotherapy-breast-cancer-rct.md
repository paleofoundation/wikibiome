# Synbiotics Reduce Chemotherapy-Induced Side Effects in Breast Cancer

## Key Claims

- Synbiotics significantly reduced severity of abnormal defecation (P=0.005) and fatigue (P<0.001) at 8 weeks
- First clinical trial on synbiotics for reducing chemotherapy side effects in breast cancer
