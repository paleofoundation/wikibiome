# A Low Aromatic Amino-Acid Diet Improves Renal Function in CKD Mice

## Key Claims

- Low aromatic amino acid diet (LA-AAD) reduces production of uremic toxins p-cresyl sulfate and indoxyl sulfate
- Both low protein diet and LA-AAD reduce proteinuria, kidney fibrosis, and inflammation
- LA-AAD is as effective as low protein diet with lower risk of malnutrition
- Free fractions of PCS and IS significantly decreased by LA-AAD

## Methods & Findings

Mouse model of chemically induced [[chronic-kidney-disease]]. Three diets compared over six weeks: normoproteic, low protein, and LA-AAD.
