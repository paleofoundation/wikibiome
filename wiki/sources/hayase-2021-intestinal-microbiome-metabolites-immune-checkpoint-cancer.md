# Intestinal Microbiome and Microbial Metabolites in Immune Checkpoint Immunotherapy

## Key Claims

- Intestinal microbiome can modulate ICI responses via the host immune system
- FMT can lead to ICI therapy responses in previously refractory patients
- Specific microbes (Bacteroides fragilis, Bifidobacterium, Faecalibacterium prausnitzii) associated with favorable ICI responses
