# Causal Associations of Gut Microbiota and Plasma Metabolites with Ovarian Cancer

## Key Claims

- Seven risk factor and five protective gut microbes identified for OC
- Caffeic acid, sphingomyelin, and ceramide metabolites were OC risk factors
- Phenylalanine and butyric acid metabolites were protective

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: Two-sample MR; network pharmacology and molecular docking validation
- **Data**: MiBioGen (18,340), Dutch Microbiome Project (7,738); 1,400 plasma metabolites; OCAC (25,509 OC cases)
