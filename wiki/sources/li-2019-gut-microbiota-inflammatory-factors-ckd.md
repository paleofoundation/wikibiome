# Gut Microbiota Alterations and Inflammatory Factors in CKD

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients show significant reduction in microbial richness and structural changes
- Akkermansia abundance significantly decreased in CKD (3.08 vs 0.67%)
- Akkermansia negatively correlated with interleukin-10 production
- Parasutterella, Lactobacillus, Desulfovibrio correlated with CKD disease-severity indicators

## Methods & Findings

16S rDNA pyrosequencing of fecal microbiota from 50 CKD and 22 healthy control subjects.
