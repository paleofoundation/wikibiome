# Socioeconomic Status and CKD: A Meta-Analysis

## Key Claims

- Lower income most closely associated with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] prevalence and progression (OR 1.34)
- Lower education significantly associated with CKD prevalence
- Combined SES inversely associated with ESRD progression

## Methods & Findings

Meta-analysis of 43 articles using random effects model.
