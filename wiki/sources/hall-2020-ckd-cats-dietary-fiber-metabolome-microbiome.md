# CKD in Cats Alters Metabolome and Microbiome Response to Dietary Fiber

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] cats had higher creatinine, urea, and microbial tryptophan metabolites at baseline
- CKD cats more resistant to microbiome OTU changes from fiber source than healthy cats
- Lower concentrations of phenolic uremic toxins in CKD cats after scFOS

## Methods & Findings

Split-plot design: 10 healthy + 10 CKD cats (IRIS stage 1-2). Two fiber treatments (scFOS vs apple pomace) for 4 weeks each.
