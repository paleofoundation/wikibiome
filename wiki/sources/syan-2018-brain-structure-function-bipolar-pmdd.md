# Syan 2018 — Brain Structure and Function in Comorbid Bipolar and PMDD

## Key Findings

- **BD+PMDD is a neurobiologically distinct phenotype** from BD-only, with unique structural and functional brain signatures across the menstrual cycle ([[syan-2018-brain-structure-function-bipolar-pmdd]], case-control, n=85).
- **Resting-state functional connectivity (Rs-FC):** BD+PMDD showed increased connectivity between L-hippocampus and R-frontal cortex, and decreased connectivity between R-hippocampus and R-premotor cortex, compared to BD-only (FDR-corrected) ([[syan-2018-brain-structure-function-bipolar-pmdd]]).
- **Cortical thickness:** BD+PMDD showed decreased thickness in L-superior frontal, L-pericaicarine, L-superior parietal, R-middle temporal, R-rostral middle frontal, L-superior frontal; increased thickness in L-superior temporal gyrus compared to BD ([[syan-2018-brain-structure-function-bipolar-pmdd]]).
- **Subcortical volumes:** Left caudate volume increased in late luteal phase in BD+PMDD (p_CORR <0.05) — not seen in other groups ([[syan-2018-brain-structure-function-bipolar-pmdd]]).
- Hormone levels (E2, ALLO, progesterone) were within normal physiological range for all groups — confirming that **differential sensitivity**, not abnormal levels, drives the comorbidity ([[syan-2018-brain-structure-function-bipolar-pmdd]]).
- MADRS scores positively correlated with Rs-FC between R-hippocampus and R-frontal cortex (r=0.46, p=0.037) ([[syan-2018-brain-structure-function-bipolar-pmdd]]).

## Methods

Four-group design: CTRL (n=25), PMDD (n=20), BD (n=21), BD+PMDD (n=19). 3T MRI at mid-follicular and late luteal phases. Rs-FC (seed-based, 14 seed regions), cortical thickness (FreeSurfer), subcortical volume analysis. Hormonal analysis: E2, DHEAS, ALLO. Euthymic for ≥2 months. DRSP-confirmed PMDD diagnosis.
