# Sampah & Hackam 2021 — Prenatal Immunity and Pathophysiology of NEC

## Key Findings

- **TLR4 is the central regulator of NEC.** The premature intestine over-expresses TLR4, making it hypersensitive to LPS from Gram-negative Proteobacteria and triggering epithelial apoptosis.
- **Gut–brain axis.** TLR4 activation in brain microglia produces dysmyelination and white-matter injury — a mechanistic link between NEC and cerebral palsy.
- **Preterm dysbiosis baseline.** Review compiles the reduced Firmicutes/Bacteroidetes and Proteobacteria dominance pattern across cohorts.
- Iron supplementation is flagged as a potential modifier via its effect on siderophore-producing Enterobacteriaceae.
