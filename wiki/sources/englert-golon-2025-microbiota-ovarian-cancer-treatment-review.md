# Potential Role of Microbiota in Ovarian Cancer Treatment

## Key Claims

- Gut, vaginal, and uterine microbiota play critical roles in responses to OC treatments
- Patients with favorable microbiota profiles show better immunotherapy response
- Microbiota represent potential biomarkers and therapeutic targets in OC
