# Key Claims

- **Metformin exerts anti-diabetic effects partly through gut microbiome modulation**
- **Gut microbiome-for-type-2-diabetes research** at University of Gothenburg/Metabogen is advancing understanding of drug-microbiome interactions
- **Pharmacomicrobiomics** is an emerging field relevant to diabetes therapeutics
