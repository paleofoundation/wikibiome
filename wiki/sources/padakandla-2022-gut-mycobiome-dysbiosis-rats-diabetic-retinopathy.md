# Key Claims

- **Mycobiomes of diabetic (DM) and diabetic retinopathy change (DRC) rats** separated into distinct clusters from controls
- **Pathogenic fungi increase** may contribute to inflammatory status in both diabetic and retinal change rats
- **Beta-diversity analysis** separated mycobiomes of DM and DRC from controls
- **Mycobiomes of diabetic rats with retinal changes** showed overlap with diabetic-only rats, suggesting shared fungal dysbiotic mechanism

# Methods & Findings

STZ-induced diabetic rat model over 4 months:

- Faecal DNA analyzed for gut mycobiomes using ITS2 sequencing
- Histology and immunohistochemistry confirmed retinal changes in DRC rats
- Discriminating genera identified between diabetic and control mycobiomes
