# Key Claims

- **VPA reduced mast cell degranulation** and cytokine release induced by L. monocytogenes
- **MC activation during L.m. infection** relies on TLR2 engagement; VPA did not affect TLR2 cell surface expression
- **VPA decreased MC activation** of critical events including intracellular Ca2+ increase, phosphorylation of p38, ERK1/2 and p65 subunit of NF-kB
- **VPA also reduced cytokine production** in response to Listeriolysin O (LLO), which activates MC by a TLR2-independent mechanism
- **VPA can modulate** the functional activity of immune cells that participate in L.m. infection control
