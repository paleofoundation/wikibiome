# I3C Alleviates Cisplatin-Induced Ovarian Damage via TGF-beta1/Smad Pathway

## Key Claims

- I3C alleviates cisplatin-induced ovarian damage by inhibiting ovarian fibrosis through TGF-beta1/Smad pathway
- Represents a potential chemoprotective strategy for preserving ovarian function during cisplatin chemotherapy
