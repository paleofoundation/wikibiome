# Cervical Cancer, Different Treatments and Importance of Bile Acids as Therapeutic Agents

## Key Claims

- Synthetic derivatives of ursodeoxycholic acid and chenodeoxycholic acid (HS-1183, HS-1199, HS-1200) can treat cervical cancer by inhibiting cell proliferation and inducing apoptosis
- Platinum compounds are used as catalysts in cervical cancer therapy via bile acid complexes
- Bile acid-metal complexes with platinum, zinc, [[nickel]], and copper represent a novel approach to gynecological cancer therapy

- **Study type**: Review of bile acid applications in cervical cancer treatment
