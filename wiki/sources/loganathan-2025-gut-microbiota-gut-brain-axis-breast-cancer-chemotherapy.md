# Gut Microbiota and the Gut-Brain Axis in Cancer Chemotherapy Patients

## Key Claims

- Firmicutes highly abundant in both chemotherapy and cancer-free groups
- Pseudomonas and Akkermansia decreased while Ruminococcus and Allistipes increased during treatment
- KEGG pathways enriched in treatment included two-component system and tyrosine metabolism
