# Circulating Bile Acids and Prevalent CKD in Type 2 Diabetes

## Key Claims

- Higher total primary bile acids associated with lower odds of [[chronic-kidney-disease]] (OR 0.78)
- Cholate, chenodeoxycholate, glycocholate, glycochenodeoxycholate all inversely associated with CKD
- No significant relationship between secondary bile acids and CKD
- Unconjugated primary BAs and glycine-conjugates protective against CKD in T2DM

## Methods & Findings

Cross-sectional study of 1234 newly diagnosed T2DM patients. UPLC-MS/MS measurement of circulating BAs.
