# Arsenic in Brown Rice: Do the Benefits Outweigh the Risks?

## Key Findings
- Brown rice contains approximately 10x higher arsenic concentrations than white rice due to bran layer retention
- Population-level arsenic exposure from brown rice consumption poses non-trivial health risk, particularly in high-rice-diet populations
- Risk-benefit analysis challenges the blanket nutritional recommendation to substitute brown rice for white rice
