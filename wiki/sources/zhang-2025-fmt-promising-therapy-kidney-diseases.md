# FMT Is a Promising Therapy for Kidney Diseases

## Key Claims

- FMT targets gut-kidney axis by inhibiting RAS, attenuating inflammation, and restoring intestinal barrier
- FMT has shown promise in diabetic nephropathy, IgA nephropathy, membranous nephropathy, and FSGS
- Most studies remain preclinical with limited clinical trial data
- Future research should incorporate AI for personalized FMT intervention
