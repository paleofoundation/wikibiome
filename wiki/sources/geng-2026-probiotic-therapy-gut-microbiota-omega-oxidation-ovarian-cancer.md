# Probiotic Therapy Protects Gut Microbiota and Activates Omega-Oxidation After OC Surgery

## Key Claims

- Probiotic treatment restored gut microbial diversity and reduced [[dysbiosis]] in post-surgical OC patients
- Increased Alistipes, Roseburia, and Ruminococcus with probiotic treatment
- Probiotics upregulated PPARalpha, CYP4F3, and CYP4A10, activating omega-oxidation pathway
- Combined SCFAs treatment further enhanced effects
