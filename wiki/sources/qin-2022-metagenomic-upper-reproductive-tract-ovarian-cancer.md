# Metagenomic Analysis of Upper Reproductive Tract Microbiome in OC

## Key Claims

- OC can alter genital microbiota in a specific way with clinical values
- OC had a distinct microbiota signature; tubal ligation affects microbial distribution
- No different species between platinum-sensitive and platinum-resistant ovarian tissue surfaces
- PPPM framework for microbiota in OC treatment proposed
