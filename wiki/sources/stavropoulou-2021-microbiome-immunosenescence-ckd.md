# Microbiome, Immunosenescence, and CKD

## Key Claims

- Aging followed by specific shifts in human microbiome predisposing to inflammation and disease
- Multiple renal diseases associated with gut [[dysbiosis]]
- Immune system, environment, medication, diet, and genetics determine microbiome aging

## Methods & Findings

Review of gut microbiota-kidney axis pathways in aging and [[chronic-kidney-disease]].
