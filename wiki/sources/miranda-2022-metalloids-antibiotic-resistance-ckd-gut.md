# Metal(loid)s and Antibiotic Resistance in CKD Gut Bacteria

## Key Claims

- [[lead]] and arsenic-resistant bacteria found in stage 3 [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients
- Pathogen-related Firmicutes and Proteobacteria observed in CKD gut
- Metal resistance genes (cadA, arsC) for [[cadmium]] and arsenic detected in CKD gut bacteria
- Decrease in metal resistance markers observed in advanced CKD stages

## Methods & Findings

Fecal samples from self-reliant stage 3 CKD patients. qPCR for metal and antibiotic resistance genes.
