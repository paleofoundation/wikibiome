# Key Claims

- **First trimester gut bacterial microbiota significantly differs** among GDM groups based on unweighted UniFrac distances (p=0.003)
- **Normoglycemic women had gut microbiota** associated with higher abundance of Prevotellaceae and genus Sutterella
- **Women diagnosed later with GDM** showed distinct first-trimester bacterial and fungal patterns
- **Gut microbiota composition in early pregnancy** may detect changes linked to later GDM development

# Methods & Findings

Prospective study of 104 pregnant women (first trimester BMI median 26.73):

- Divided into 4 groups by FPG/oGTT in first and third trimesters
- Stool samples processed for bacterial 16S and fungal ITS1 sequencing (Illumina MiSeq)
- SCFA levels correlated with gut microbiota composition
