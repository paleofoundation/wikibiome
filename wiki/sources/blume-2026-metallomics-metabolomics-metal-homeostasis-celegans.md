# Combined Metallomics and Metabolomics Reveal Metal Homeostasis Impact

## Key Findings

- **Cross-metal regulation**: Zinc regulates the **free (labile) metal species** of iron and manganese — demonstrating that metals don't operate independently but form **regulatory networks** where one metal's status affects others [[blume-2026-metallomics-metabolomics-metal-homeostasis-celegans]], animal-model.
- **Free vs. total**: It's the **free metal pool**, not total metal content, that drives biological effects. This validates the [[mis-metallation]] concept — what matters is which metal is available to bind which protein, not bulk concentrations.
- **Iron → ferroptosis**: Iron exposure impairs energy metabolism through [[ferroptosis]]-related mechanisms, confirming the iron-lipid peroxidation-cell death pathway.
