# Bacteriome, Mycobiome and Metabolome Alterations in PCOS

## Key Claims

- Metabolite-derived model is more accurate than microbe-based model for discriminating PCOS
- Hyperandrogenemia plays central role in dysbiosis of intestinal microecology in PCOS
- Uniquely higher abundance of fungal genus Mortierella in PCOS patients
