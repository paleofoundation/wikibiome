# Chen 2024 — Causal Gut Microbiota in Male ED (MR)

## Key Findings

- **Ruminococcaceae UCG013** protective (OR = 0.761, 95% CI 0.626–0.926) — replicates [[zhang-2023-causal-gut-microbiota-ed-mr]].
- **Lachnospiraceae NC2004** genus-level risk factor (OR = 1.189).
- **Family Lachnospiraceae** OR = 1.264 — confirms family-level risk with genus-level resolution.
- Independent MR replication strengthens causal inference for the gut–penis axis.

## Methods

Two-sample MR using MiBioGen and ED GWAS. Overlapping underlying consortia with Zhang 2023; serves as methodological replication with different taxa-level resolution.
