# Gut Mycobiome in CKD Patients and Immunological Profiles

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] cohort had significantly higher gut mycobiome richness and diversity
- Lower abundances of Candida, Bjerkandera, Rhodotorula, Ganoderma in CKD
- Higher Saccharomyces in CKD cohort
- Kappa light chain positively associated with Saccharomyces, negatively with Ganoderma

## Methods & Findings

92 CKD patients and BMI-matched healthy controls. ITS sequencing and immunoturbidimetric immunological assays.
