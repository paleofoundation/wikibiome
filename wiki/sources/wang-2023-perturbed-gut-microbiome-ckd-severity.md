# Perturbed Gut Microbiome and Metabolomes Associated with CKD Severity

## Key Claims

- 26 microbial species significantly changed in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients, 18 changed with disease progression
- Ruminococcus bromii and fecal hydroquinone were main contributors to integrated CKD severity model
- Model classified CKD severity (AUC >0.9) better than serum creatinine alone

## Methods & Findings

Metagenomics and untargeted metabolomics in 68 CKD patients (varying severities) + 20 healthy controls.
