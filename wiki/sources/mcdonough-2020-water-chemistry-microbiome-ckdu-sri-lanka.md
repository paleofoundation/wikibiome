# Water Chemistry and Microbiome in CKDu-Endemic Sri Lanka

## Key Claims

- Frequent presence of cyanotoxin-producing Microcystis in household well water
- Total microbial content of drinking water influences gut microbiome composition
- Bacteria previously found in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patient gut also present in household wells

## Methods & Findings

Chemical and microbial assessment of 30 household wells in Medawachchiya District, Sri Lanka.
