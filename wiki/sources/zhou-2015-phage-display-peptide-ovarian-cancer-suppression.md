# Phage Display Screening Identifies Novel Peptide to Suppress Ovarian Cancer

## Key Claims

- Peptide1 (SWQIGGN) showed preferential binding to OC cells and inhibited proliferation, migration, invasion, adhesion
- In vivo, led to lower tumorigenicity and inhibited tumor growth and metastasis
