# Key Claims

- **Non-personalized dietary approaches** (GFCF, KD, probiotics) may not be effective for all ASD individuals
- **Not all ASD children show immune reactivity** to dietary proteins in wheat and milk
- **Dietary aquaporins** that resemble human aquaporins may elicit antibody reactivity in genetically susceptible individuals
- **Three-step personalized treatment plan** proposed using systems approach connecting dietary, immune, and neuroautoimmune findings
- **Gut-immune-endocrine-brain axis** framework is more comprehensive than gut-brain axis alone
