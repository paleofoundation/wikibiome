# NSAIDs/Paracetamol Adverse Drug Reactions and Gut Microbiota

## Key Claims

- NSAIDs more commonly associated with GI bleeding, allergic reactions, and renal failure
- Gut microbiota alterations may play pivotal role in occurrence of ADRs
- Relevant to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] pharmacotherapy management

## Methods & Findings

Retrospective study of 897 ADR patients (2015-2021).
