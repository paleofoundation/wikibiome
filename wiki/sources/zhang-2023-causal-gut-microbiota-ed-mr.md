# Zhang 2023 — Causal Gut Microbiota and Erectile Dysfunction

## Key Findings

- **Family *Lachnospiraceae*** causally increases ED risk (OR = 1.265) — a surprising finding, given Lachnospiraceae are generally considered beneficial SCFA producers.
- **Ruminococcaceae UCG013** is protective (OR = 0.770).
- **Senegalimassilia** increases ED risk (OR = 1.320).
- Establishes a causal gut–penis axis signature via MR.

## Methods

Two-sample Mendelian randomization using MiBioGen microbiome GWAS and ED GWAS summary statistics (223,805 controls). Inverse-variance-weighted with sensitivity analyses.
