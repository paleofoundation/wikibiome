# Dietary Fiber Intake and Cognitive Impairment in Older CKD Patients

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients scored lower on CERAD-WL, CERAD-DR, AFT, and DSST cognitive tests
- High dietary fiber intake eliminated CERAD-WL and DSST differences between CKD and non-CKD
- Positive linear relationship between dietary fiber and AFT score in older CKD patients

## Methods & Findings

Cross-sectional study using NHANES 2011-2014 data. 2461 older adults (32% CKD). Cognitive tests and dietary records.
