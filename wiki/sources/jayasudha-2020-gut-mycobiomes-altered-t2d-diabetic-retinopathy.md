# Key Claims

- **Dysbiotic changes observed in gut mycobiomes** of T2DM and DR patients vs healthy controls at phyla and genera levels
- **Mycobiomes of HC, T2DM, and DR** could be discriminated by heat map, beta diversity, and LEfSe analyses
- **Spearman correlation of fungal genera** indicated more negative correlation in HC vs T2DM and DR mycobiomes
- **Dysbiosis demonstrated at both phyla and genera levels** between all three groups

# Methods & Findings

ITS2 region sequencing from gut mycobiomes: HC, T2DM, and DR groups:

- Data analyzed using QIIME and R software
- Significant differences at phyla and genera levels between all groups
- Novel insights into role of gut fungi in diabetes and its complications
