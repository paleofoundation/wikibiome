# Chemical Characterization of Baby Food Consumed in Italy

## Key Findings
- Multiple baby food products sold in Italy exceed EU regulatory limits for heavy metals
- Cadmium and lead are the most prevalent contaminants across product categories
- Infant exposure is disproportionately concerning due to low body weight and developing organ systems
