# Changes in Gut Microbial Community upon CKD

## Key Claims

- Significant differences in evenness between [[chronic-kidney-disease]] and healthy groups at phylum level
- F/B ratio higher in CKD suggesting energy absorption imbalance
- Ralstonia and Porphyromonas increased in CKD and negatively correlated with eGFR
- Different metabolic pathways between CKD and healthy groups

## Methods & Findings

Bioinformatics analysis of 315 samples from NCBI database comparing CKD and healthy groups.
