# Key Claims

- **SSRIs** are first-line antidepressants for PPD; commonly used in clinical practice include paroxetine, sertraline, fluoxetine, citalopram
- **Brexanolone** (allopregnanolone) is a novel rapid-acting oral treatment for [[postpartum-depression]]; FDA-approved intravenous formulation
- **Zuranolone** is an investigational positive allosteric modulator of GABA-A receptors currently in clinical development for oral treatment
- **Non-pharmacological treatments** encompass psychotherapy, physical therapy, music and exercise therapy
- **30-50% of MDD patients** exhibit only partial responses to multiple antidepressants

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Comprehensive review of PPD treatment modalities; systematic search of Chinese and international databases
- **Key coverage**: Pharmacological (SSRIs, SNRIs, TCAs, brexanolone, zuranolone), non-pharmacological, and combined treatment approaches
