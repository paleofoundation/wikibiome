## Key Findings

- Prospective matched case-control study at PGIMER Chandigarh; preterm infants (26–32 weeks gestation, birth weight <1500 g) admitted to NICU. Stool samples collected during the first month of life, gestational-age/postnatal-age matched controls (n=12 NEC vs 12 controls after QC).
- No significant differences in alpha diversity (observed OTUs, Shannon) or beta diversity (Bray-Curtis, Jaccard) between NEC and controls — consistent with other preterm NEC studies where functional shifts precede broad diversity changes.
- **Enterobacteriaceae** significantly over-abundant in NEC (linear regression coefficient 4.11, p=0.003, adjusted p=0.034). Genus-level: **Escherichia** and **Klebsiella** increased; **Enterococcus** decreased in NEC.
- LEfSe: **Klebsiella pneumoniae** and **K. quasipneumoniae** enriched in NEC; unclassified **Acinetobacter** enriched in non-NEC. 82% of NEC cases had either Escherichia or Klebsiella present vs 33.3% of controls.
- Functional (SEED/STAMP) analysis — pathways significantly enriched in NEC: **type IV secretion systems (T4SS), conjugative transfer, LPS O-antigen (enterobacterial common antigen), quorum sensing in Yersinia, L-rhamnose pathway, and iron transport systems (ABC transporters, Shikimate kinase SK3)**. Folate biosynthesis and lactose utilization were reduced in NEC.
- First shotgun metagenomic NEC study from India — externally validates LPS/iron-acquisition functional signature seen in Casaburi 2022, Pammi 2017, Zhou 2015.

## Relevance to Cureva

- Supports the NEC signature layer: Enterobacteriaceae (Klebsiella + E. coli) dominance with iron-acquisition and LPS gene enrichment as functional NEC features.
- Iron transporter enrichment reinforces the STOP signal against early iron supplementation in preterm infants at NEC risk.
- Loss of folate biosynthesis and lactose utilization genes supports the ecological engineering approach (restoring missing commensal functions, not just suppressing pathogens).

## Contradictions / Tensions

- Unlike some studies, alpha/beta diversity were NOT significantly different — consistent with NEC being a functional/species-specific shift rather than a diversity collapse. Confirms shotgun metagenomics reveals signal invisible to 16S.

## Open Questions

- Small sample size (n=12 vs 12); replication in larger Indian/South Asian cohorts needed.
- Longitudinal sampling to track T4SS/iron transporter enrichment before NEC onset not performed here.
