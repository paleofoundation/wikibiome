# The C18:3n6/C22:4n6 Ratio as a Lipid Marker of CKD Progression

## Key Claims

- C18:3n6/C22:4n6 ratio decreases with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] progression and is a novel disease marker
- Decreased total PUFA and increased MUFA concentrations in CKD
- Reduction in EPA and DHA with CKD progression

## Methods & Findings

149 CKD patients + 43 controls. Gas chromatography for 30 fatty acids and derivatives.
