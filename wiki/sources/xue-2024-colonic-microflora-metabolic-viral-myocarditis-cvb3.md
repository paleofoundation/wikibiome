# Altered Colonic Microflora and Its Metabolic Profile in Mice with Acute Viral Myocarditis Induced by Coxsackievirus B3

## Summary

Mouse model study examining gut microflora and metabolic changes during acute viral myocarditis (AVMC) induced by coxsackievirus B3 (CVB3). Combined 16S rRNA sequencing with UHPLC-MS/MS untargeted metabolomics profiling of colonic contents.

## Key Findings

### Microbiota Alterations in AVMC
- Significant reduction in gut microflora richness (ACE, Chao1) and diversity (Shannon, Simpson) in AVMC mice
- Increased *Proteobacteria* and decreased *Cyanobacteria* and *Desulfobacterota*
- Top discriminative genera: *Escherichia-Shigella*, *Lactobacillus*, *Clostridium_sensu_stricto_1*, *Prevotellaceae_UCG-001*, *Odoribacter*
- Increased *Escherichia-Shigella* and *Lactobacillus*; decreased proportions of multiple genera in AVMC group

### Metabolomic Changes
- 198 differential metabolites identified (79 upregulated, 119 downregulated)
- Lipids and lipid-like molecules accounted for largest proportion
- Lipid metabolism pathway significantly enriched: Traumatic acid (TA), Alpha-Linolenic acid (ALA), EPA, and DHA as key differential metabolites
- Positive correlations between TA levels and *Anaerotruncus* and *[[bilophila|Bilophila]]* abundance
- Positive correlations between EPA levels and *Clostridium_sensu_stricto_1* abundance

### Gut-Heart Axis in Viral Myocarditis
- CVB3 infects through gut pathway; gut serves as primary portal of entry
- Gut barrier dysfunction enables viral and bacterial translocation to heart
- AVMC mice showed distinct bacterial community composition from controls (PCoA, NMDS)
- Cardiac pathological scores significantly elevated in AVMC group

## Relevance to Gut-Metal-Microbiome Axis

1. **Viral microbiota and CVD** - CVB3-induced myocarditis demonstrates direct viral contribution to [[cardiovascular-disease]] via gut-heart axis
2. **Dysbiosis amplifies viral pathology** - Gut microbiome disruption during viral infection may exacerbate cardiac damage
3. **Metabolomic signatures** - Lipid metabolism alterations provide potential biomarkers for viral myocarditis progression
