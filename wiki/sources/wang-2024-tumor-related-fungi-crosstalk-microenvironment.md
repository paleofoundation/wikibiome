# Tumor-Related Fungi and Crosstalk with Gut Fungi in the Tumor Microenvironment

## Key Claims

- Cancer type-specific fungal profiles have been found in different tumor tissues
- Fungi influence tumorigenesis through host immunity, bioactive metabolites, microbiome interactions, and epigenetic factors
- Fungal mechanisms include host recognition, immune regulation, biofilm formation, toxin production
