# Key Claims

- **Synbiotics and dietary regulation** represent a new strategy for diabetes management through gut microbiota modulation
- **Metagenomic sequencing** enables detailed functional characterization of microbiome changes during intervention
- **Combined dietary and probiotic approaches** may be more effective than either alone
