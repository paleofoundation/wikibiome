# Cai 2022 — Gut Microbiota and Male Reproduction

## Key Findings

- **H2S as erection effector:** sulfate-reducing bacteria (*Desulfovibrio*, *Desulfobacter*) produce hydrogen sulfide, which supplements the NO/cGMP pathway in corpus cavernosum relaxation — a parallel gaseous-signaling mechanism to NO.
- **Vitamin and cofactor synthesis:** gut microbiota generate vitamins A, K, and folate required for spermatogenesis.
- **GABA from gut Bacteroides and E. coli** promotes sperm capacitation.
- **Zinc and calcium** feature as essential micronutrients for sperm function — linking metallomics to male reproductive health.

## Methods

Narrative review of microbiota–male-reproductive axis across spermatogenesis, erectile function, and libido.
