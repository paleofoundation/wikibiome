# Causal Association between Oral Microbiome and Chronic Kidney Disease

## Key Claims

- Veillonella species causally associated with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] (OR = 0.96)
- Order Fusobacteriales and Rothia species causally associated with urinary albumin-to-creatinine ratio
- Bacteroidales and micronuciformis causally associated with CKDi25
- Streptococcus species causally associated with dialysis (OR = 0.82)

## Methods & Findings

Two-sample MR using GWAS summary statistics from the GWAS Catalog and CKDGen Consortium. IVW, MR-Egger, weighted median analyses.
