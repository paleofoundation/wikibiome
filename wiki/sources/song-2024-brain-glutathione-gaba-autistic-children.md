# Key Claims

- **No significant differences** in cerebral GSH or GABA levels between ASD (n=52) and TDC (n=49) groups across all examined brain regions
- **HERMES MR spectroscopy at 3T** used for comprehensive multi-region analysis
- **Regions examined**: primary motor cortex (SM1), thalamus, medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC), supplementary motor area (SMA)
- **Concentrations of GSH** (antioxidant and neuromodulator) were not altered in ASD children aged 8-12 years
- Important **null finding** challenging assumptions about cerebral GSH and GABA dysfunction in ASD
