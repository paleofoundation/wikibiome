# Key Findings

- **Two distinct microbiota clusters** identified in breast tumor tissue: one enriched in **Proteobacteria**, the other in Actinobacteria and Firmicutes [[thompson-2017-breast-cancer-microbiota-host-gene-expression]], cross-sectional, n=668.
- **Listeria** associated with expression profiles of genes involved in **epithelial-mesenchymal transition (EMT)** — a process critical for cancer metastasis [[thompson-2017-breast-cancer-microbiota-host-gene-expression]].
- **Haemophilus influenzae** significantly associated with the tumor microenvironment, possibly residing in surrounding stromal material [[thompson-2017-breast-cancer-microbiota-host-gene-expression]].
- Microbiota composition associated with host gene expression profiles, indicating microbial contribution to the tumor microenvironment.
