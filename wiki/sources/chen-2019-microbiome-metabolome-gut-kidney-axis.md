# Microbiome-Metabolome and the Gut-Kidney Axis

## Key Claims

- Disruption of gut microbiota leads to intestinal [[dysbiosis]], barrier dysfunction, and bacterial translocation
- Excess uremic toxins (indoxyl sulphate, p-cresyl sulphate, TMAO) implicated in [[chronic-kidney-disease]] progression
- Probiotic, prebiotic, and symbiotic measures discussed for re-establishing symbiosis

## Methods & Findings

Review covering CKD, IgA nephropathy, nephrolithiasis, hypertension, and kidney injury.
