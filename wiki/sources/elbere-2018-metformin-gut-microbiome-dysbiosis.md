# Metformin and Gut Microbiome Dysbiosis

## Key Claims

- Significant reduction in inner diversity of gut microbiota already 24 hours after metformin
- Increase in Escherichia-Shigella after metformin treatment
- Metformin has immediate effect on healthy human gut microbiome

## Methods & Findings

Exploratory longitudinal study of 18 healthy individuals treated with metformin for 7 days. 16S rRNA sequencing. Relevant to [[chronic-kidney-disease]] pharmacotherapy.
