# I3C Synergistically Sensitises Ovarian Cancer to Bortezomib

## Key Claims

- I3C sensitised OC cell lines to bortezomib through potent synergistic mechanisms
- Combination treatment led to cell cycle arrest and apoptosis through ER stress, cytoskeleton, chemoresistance disruption
- In vivo, co-treatment significantly inhibited tumour growth
