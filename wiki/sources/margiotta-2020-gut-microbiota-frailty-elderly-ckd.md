# Gut Microbiota Composition and Frailty in Elderly Patients with Chronic Kidney Disease

## Key Claims

- No alpha diversity differences between CKD, frail, and control groups
- Higher abundance of potentially noxious bacteria (Citrobacter, Coprobacillus) and lower saccharolytic/butyrate-producing bacteria in [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Mogibacteriaceae positively related to inflammatory indices in CKD
- Akkermansia, Ruminococcus, Eubacterium negatively related to inflammation

## Methods & Findings

64 CKD patients (stage 3b-4) categorized as frail or not-frail, plus 15 controls. DGGE and 16S qPCR analysis.
