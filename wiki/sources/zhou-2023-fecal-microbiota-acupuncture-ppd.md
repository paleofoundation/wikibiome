# Key Claims

- **47.27% of PPD patients responded** to acupuncture treatment; 12.5% recovered without treatment at 8-week follow-up
- **Paraprevotella and Desulfovibrio** were significantly enriched in acupuncture responders and may serve as predictive biomarkers (AUC 0.76 and 0.66)
- **Beta-diversity** was significantly higher in non-responders than responders
- Gut microbiota composition at baseline can predict therapeutic response to acupuncture in [[postpartum-depression]]

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: 79 PPD patients enrolled; 55 treated with acupuncture, 24 untreated controls; baseline fecal samples analyzed by 16S rRNA sequencing; HAMD-17 scores at baseline and 8 weeks
- **Setting**: Shenzhen, China
- **Key implication**: Microbiome-based precision medicine for selecting PPD treatment modalities
