# Dietary Arsenic Intake and Subsequent Risk of Cancer: The Japanese Study

## Key Findings
- Dietary arsenic intake shows a dose-dependent relationship with overall cancer risk in a large Japanese cohort
- Rice is the primary dietary source of arsenic exposure in the study population
- Even within regulatory limits, chronic dietary arsenic exposure contributes measurably to cancer incidence

## Relevance to WikiBiome
