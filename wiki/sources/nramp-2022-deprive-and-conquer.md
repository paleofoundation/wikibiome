# Nramp 2022 — Deprive and Conquer

## Key Findings
- **Nramp1 (SLC11A1) pumps Mn²⁺ and Fe²⁺ out of the phagosome**, starving engulfed pathogens of the metals they need for antioxidant defense and metabolism [[nramp-2022-deprive-and-conquer]]
- Nramp polymorphisms in humans are associated with susceptibility to intracellular pathogens (Mycobacterium, Salmonella, Leishmania)
- The transporter operates at the intersection of innate immunity and metal ecology — it is nutritional immunity enacted at the organelle level

## Relevance to WikiBiome
- Foundational for Primitive 2: Nramp is the molecular proof that low metal levels in infection contexts reflect active host defense, not deficiency
- Connects host genetics (Nramp polymorphisms) to differential susceptibility to metal-dependent pathogens
