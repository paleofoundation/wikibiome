# Key Claims

- **Metformin significantly changed the relative abundance of 11 bacterial genera** during intervention but changes returned to baseline after treatment cessation
- **Reduced abundance of Intestinibacter and Clostridium** spp. observed during metformin treatment
- **Pre-treatment gut microbiota** was related to gastrointestinal adverse effects during metformin
- **27 healthy young Danish men** (18-35 years, non-diabetic) studied in 18-week crossover trial
- Changes were **reversible** -- microbiota returned to baseline after wash-out period

# Methods & Findings

**Non-blinded, one-armed intervention study** with 18-week crossover design.

- Metformin up to 1g twice daily for 6-week intervention period
- Fecal samples at 9 time points; 16S rRNA gene sequencing
- 11 bacterial genera changed during intervention
- GI symptoms related to pre-treatment microbiota composition
- All changes reversed after treatment cessation
