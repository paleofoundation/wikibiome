# The Updated Landscape of Tumor Microenvironment and Drug Repurposing

## Key Claims

- TME comprises six specialized niches: hypoxic, immune, metabolism, acidic, innervated, and mechanical
- Conventional drugs (aspirin, celecoxib, metformin, statins) are viable candidates for combination cancer therapy
- Drug repurposing provides a compelling strategy for enhancing cancer treatment options
