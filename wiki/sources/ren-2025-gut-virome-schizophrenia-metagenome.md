# Key Claims

- **171 vOTUs differed significantly** between schizophrenia patients and controls, with 124 enriched in SCZ
- **Siphoviridae and Flandersviridae** were the main enriched viral families in SCZ
- **Disease-specific virus-bacteria associations** with Akkermansia and Clostridia found in SCZ
- **Random forest classifier based on virome features** achieved AUC of 93.2%, outperforming bacterial model
- **External validation using ASD cohorts** yielded lower AUCs (61.2-67.0%), suggesting disease specificity

# Methods & Findings

**Cross-sectional metagenomics study** of 171 individuals (90 SCZ patients and 81 controls).

- Analyzed fecal metagenomic data using Chinese Gut Virus Catalog
- Assessed virome diversity, virus-bacteria correlations, and diagnostic potential
- Follow-up samples assessed antipsychotic treatment impact on virome
- Antipsychotic therapy altered specific viral taxa without broadly reshaping virome structure
