# Aggarwal & Kumaraswami 2022 — Manganese Homeostasis in Streptococcal Pathogenesis

## Key Findings
- **Streptococci are manganese-centric pathogens** that require Mn for oxidative stress defense, metabolic enzymes, and virulence factor expression [[aggarwal-2022-manganese-homeostasis-streptococcal-pathogenesis]]
- Manganese mismetallation — Mn displacing other metals in host enzymes — is itself a virulence strategy, disrupting host immune signaling (Primitive 3)
- Host calprotectin sequesters Mn at infection sites, but streptococci deploy high-affinity Mn importers (MntABC, MntH) to overcome starvation

## Relevance to WikiBiome
- Core example of Primitive 1: manganese availability selects for Mn-dependent streptococci over competitors
- Mismetallation as an offensive weapon expands the concept beyond passive toxic metal entry — pathogens weaponize metal displacement
