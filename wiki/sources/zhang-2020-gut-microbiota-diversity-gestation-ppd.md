# Key Claims

- **Third-trimester gut microbiota diversity** (32-39 weeks gestation) may predict [[postpartum-depression]] risk
- Prenatal gut microbiome composition during late pregnancy serves as a potential early biomarker for PPD
- Links gestational [[dysbiosis]] to postpartum mental health outcomes

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Prospective study analyzing gut microbiota diversity in pregnant women during third trimester and following up for PPD symptoms
- **Setting**: Guangzhou Women's and Children's Medical Center, China
- **Status**: Preprint (Research Square), posted October 2020
