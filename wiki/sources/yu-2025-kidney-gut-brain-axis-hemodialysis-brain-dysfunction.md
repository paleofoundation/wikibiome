# Kidney-Gut-Brain Axis in Hemodialysis Brain Dysfunction

## Key Claims

- MHD patients experience brain dysfunction from uremic toxins, hemodynamic changes, and intestinal [[dysbiosis]]
- Cognitive impairment incidence in MHD is 30-60.9%
- Brain dysfunction mechanisms reviewed through lens of kidney-gut-brain axis

## Methods & Findings

Review of brain dysfunction mechanisms in maintenance hemodialysis patients via kidney-gut-brain axis in [[chronic-kidney-disease]].
