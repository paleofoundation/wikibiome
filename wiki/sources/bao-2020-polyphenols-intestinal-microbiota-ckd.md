# Polyphenols Regulate Host Intestinal Microbiota in CKD Prevention

## Key Claims

- Polyphenols (anthocyanin, catechin, chlorogenic acid, resveratrol) can inhibit pathogenic bacteria
- Polyphenols help regulate intestinal microflora to immunize against [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Intestinal barrier maintenance and microbiota balance can treat pre-existing CKD
- Polyphenols may be suitable natural substitutes for antibiotics in gut regulation

## Methods & Findings

Narrative review of polyphenol effects on gut microbiota in CKD prevention and treatment.
