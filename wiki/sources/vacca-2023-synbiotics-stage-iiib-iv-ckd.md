# Synbiotics Trial in Stage IIIb-IV CKD Patients

## Key Claims

- Significant changes in microbiota profile and increase in saccharolytic metabolism in synbiotic arm
- Selective efficacy of synbiotics on stage IIIb-IV [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients demonstrated

## Methods & Findings

Randomized, single-blind, placebo-controlled pilot trial in stage IIIb-IV CKD patients.
