# Key Claims

- **Camouflaging** refers to strategies used by autistic individuals to mask or compensate for autistic traits in social situations
- Systematic review of the camouflaging literature in ASD
- Relevant to understanding diagnostic delays, particularly in females and those with higher cognitive abilities
- Camouflaging associated with mental health consequences including exhaustion and identity confusion
