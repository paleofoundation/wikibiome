# FMT from PCOS Patients Induces Metabolic Disorders and Ovarian Dysfunction

## Key Claims

- FMT from PCOS patients induced disruption of ovarian functions, insulin resistance, and obese-like phenotype in germ-free mice
- PCOS fecal microbiota enriched in Phocaeicola, Mediterraneibacter, Oscillospiraceae, Lawsonibacter
- The microbiome may govern the set point of PCOS-bearing individuals
