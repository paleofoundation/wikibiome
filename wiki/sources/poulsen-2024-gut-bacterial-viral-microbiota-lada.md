# Key Claims

- **LADA gut bacteriome resembled T2D** more than T1D in alpha and beta diversity measures
- **LADA has unique taxonomical features** distinct from both T1D and T2D, with specific genera differing in abundance
- **Virome composition reflected stability** of most dominant order Caudovirales and families Siphoviridae, Podoviridae, Inoviridae
- **Dominant family Microviridae** had unique features in LADA vs T1D and T2D
- **First characterization of gut virome** in LADA patients

# Methods & Findings

Cross-sectional study comparing LADA to T1D, T2D, and non-diabetic controls:

- 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing (variable region 4) for bacteriome
- Viral particle enrichment and metagenomics for virome
- DADA2 for amplicon sequence variants, SILVA database for annotation

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: LADA (autoimmune with insulin resistance features) may have unique zinc dysregulation combining T1D and T2D patterns
- **[[iron]]**: Virome composition (Caudovirales stability) may be influenced by iron-dependent phage-host dynamics
