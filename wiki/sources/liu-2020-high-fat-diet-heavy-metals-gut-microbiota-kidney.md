# High-Fat Diet Affects Heavy Metal Accumulation and Kidney Toxicity via Gut Microbiota

## Key Claims

- HFD mice accumulated more heavy metals in liver and kidney with worse functional damage
- Arsenic exposure increased Bacteroides, Lactobacillus, Butyricomonas, Dorea
- [[cadmium]] exposure increased Desulfovibrio, Plasmodium, Roseburia
- [[lead]] exposure increased Desulfovibrio and Faecoccus abundance

## Methods & Findings

Normal-diet and high-fat-diet mice exposed to 100 ppm As, Cd, or Pb. 16S rRNA sequencing and organ analysis.
