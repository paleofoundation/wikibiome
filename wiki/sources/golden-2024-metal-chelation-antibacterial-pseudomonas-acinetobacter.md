## Key Findings

- **Disrupting metal homeostasis disarms virulence** without directly killing bacteria — a fundamentally different mechanism from conventional antibiotics that reduces resistance selection pressure
- **[[pseudomonas-aeruginosa]] and [[acinetobacter-baumannii]]** rely on species-specific siderophore uptake systems exploitable for Trojan horse antibiotic delivery
- **Competitive [[iron]] deprivation** — chelators that outcompete bacterial siderophores — offers an alternative to bactericidal strategies, extending to [[zinc]], [[manganese]], and [[copper]] dependencies
