# Probiotics and High Fiber-Rich Diets Decline CKD Progression

## Key Claims

- Balanced diet (50% more vegetables, time-restricted eating) plus probiotics slowed [[chronic-kidney-disease]] progression
- Positive correlation between daily probiotics consumption and decreased CKD progression

## Methods & Findings

Two groups of CKD patients: Group A (modified diet + probiotics) vs Group B (probiotics only). 3-month intervention.
