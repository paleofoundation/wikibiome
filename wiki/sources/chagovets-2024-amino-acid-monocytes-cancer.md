# Specific Changes in Amino Acid Profiles in Monocytes of Cancer Patients

## Key Claims

- Citrulline, sarcosine, and glutamic acid are ovarian cancer-specific amino acids in circulating monocytes
- For ovarian cancer, aminobutyric acid was significantly elevated (AUC=1.00)
- Methyl histidine and cysteine in monocytes had strong negative correlation with lymph node metastasis in OC (r=-0.95)
- Cancer-specific metabolic programming in monocytes reflects systemic innate immunity response

## Methods/Findings

- **Study design**: Prospective multicenter study; 43 cancer patients (6 OC) and 10 healthy controls
- **Methods**: Targeted amino acid LC-MS/MS analysis of 26 amino acids in isolated CD14+ monocytes
