# Key Claims

- **5 intestinal species and 24 blood metabolites** causally associated with [[postpartum-depression]] via MR analysis
- **Prevotellaceae and Bifidobacteria** may reduce PPD risk by elevating Xanthine and 1-arachidonoylglycerophosphoinositol (LysoPI) levels
- Identifies metabolite-mediated pathways linking [[dysbiosis]] to PPD

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Two-sample MR analysis; instrumental variables from MiBioGen consortium and metabolomics GWAS; PPD SNPs from FinnGen consortium
- **Methods**: IVW, weighted median, weighted mode, MR-Egger; inverse MR and sensitivity analyses for stability
- **Key implication**: Provides theoretical basis for PPD-related biomarkers and treatment via gut microenvironment regulation

# Connections

- Metabolite-mediated causal pathways support [[gut-brain-axis]] involvement in PPD
