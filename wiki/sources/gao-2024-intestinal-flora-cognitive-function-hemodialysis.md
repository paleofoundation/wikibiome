# Intestinal Flora and Cognitive Function in Hemodialysis Patients

## Key Claims

- Roseburia significantly reduced in MHD group
- Faecalibacterium, Megamonas, Bifidobacterium positively correlated with cognitive domains
- Roseburia may be important cognitive-related taxon in MHD patients

## Methods & Findings

36 MHD patients and 18 healthy controls. Combined 16S rDNA and shotgun metagenomic sequencing.
