# Vaginal Microbiome Alterations in HPV16 Infection

## Key Claims

- HPV16-positive women showed higher Gardnerella, Peptostreptococcus, Prevotella and lower Firmicutes, Actinobacteria
- 12 genera, 17 genera, and 7 species biomarkers showed excellent predictive power (AUC up to 0.918)
- HPV16-positive vaginal microbiome enriched in metabolism and membrane transport
