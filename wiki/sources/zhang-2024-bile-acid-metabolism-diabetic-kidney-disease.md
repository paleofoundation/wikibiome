# Bile Acid Metabolism Alterations in Diabetic Kidney Disease

## Key Claims

- Step-wise alterations of 50 kinds of bile acids in plasma, feces, and urine during DKD progression
- Three kynurenine derivatives upregulated in ESRD compared to [[chronic-kidney-disease]]
- Glycochenodeoxycholic acid and 7-ketodeoxycholic acid strongly correlated with DKD clinical indicators
- Inhibiting kynurenine pathway may delay CKD-to-ESRD progression

## Methods & Findings

UPLC-MS/MS quantification of BAs in plasma, fecal, and urine samples from T2DM, DKD, and healthy controls.
