# Epidemiology of Ovarian Cancer: A Review

## Key Claims

- Epithelial OC is the most predominant pathologic subtype with five major histotypes
- GWAS identified 29 common susceptibility alleles including 14 subtype-specific alleles
- Reproductive and hormonal factors may lower risk; older age at menopause and HRT confer increased risk
- Environmental risk factors include asbestos and talc powder exposures
