# Hashimoto's Thyroiditis: Lifestyle Interventions for Finding and Treating the Root Cause

## Key Claims

- The three factors for [[thyroid-autoimmunity|thyroid autoimmunity]] are: (1) genes, (2) triggers, and (3) [[intestinal-permeability]], and while genes cannot be changed, triggers can be removed and intestinal permeability corrected
- The "Five R's of Healing Hashimoto's" framework proposes: Remove (iodine, infections, fluoride, toxins, food intolerances, stress), Repair (healing diet, broths, glutamine), Replace (B12, zinc, selenium, ferritin, thyroid hormone, digestive enzymes), Reinoculate ([[probiotics]], [[prebiotics|fermented foods]]), and Rebalance (adaptogens, curcumin, omega-3s)
- [[celiac-disease|Gluten]] and food intolerances promote [[intestinal-permeability]], making the immune system unable to recognize self cells, while poorly digested food feeds pathogenic bacteria
- Multiple diets have been reported to reverse [[hashimotos-thyroiditis]] and other autoimmune conditions, including gluten-free, iodine-free, Specific Carbohydrate Diet, GAPS diet, Paleo diet, Autoimmune Paleo diet, low-FODMAPs diet, and Body Ecology Diet
- [[prebiotics|Fermented foods]] and [[probiotics]] are recommended to reinoculate the gut with beneficial bacteria to displace pathogenic organisms
- Increasing [[dietary-fiber]] by approximately 30 g per day in women with Hashimoto's increases the rate of weight loss and improves efficacy of nutritional therapy

## Methods/Findings

- **Source type**: Book (clinical guide), not a primary research study
- **Framework**: Functional medicine approach to Hashimoto's management tailored by the author (PharmD) based on clinical experience
- **Diet recommendations**: Nutrient-dense, animal protein-inclusive (suggested 20% meat / 80% vegetable ratio); healing foods include fermented foods and gelatins; removal of reactive foods common across multiple diets
- **Supplementation**: Emphasis on correcting depletions of B12, [[zinc]], [[selenium]], [[ferritin]], and [[gut-thyroid-axis|thyroid hormone]]
- **Gut repair**: Glutamine, bone broth, and healing diet to address [[intestinal-permeability|leaky gut]]; protomorphogens as decoys for thyroid and adrenal gland repair
- **Immune rebalancing**: Anatabine, [[polyphenols|curcumin]], and omega-3 supplements; green juices and alkaline foods to support alkaline phosphatase detoxification of endotoxin
- **Stress management**: Adrenal adaptogens, blood sugar balancing, and stress reduction as key components
