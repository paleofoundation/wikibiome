# Link between Gut Microbiota Dysbiosis and CKD

## Key Claims

- Uremic toxin accumulation (IS, p-CS, TMAO) changes with [[chronic-kidney-disease]] stage and worsens in hemodialysis
- Positive shift in gut microbiota composition observed after renal transplantation
- Either gut [[dysbiosis]] or uremic toxin accumulation contributes to CKD onset and progression
- Mediterranean diet may be beneficial for CKD patients

## Methods & Findings

Narrative review of gut microbiota [[dysbiosis]] mechanisms in CKD pathophysiology.
