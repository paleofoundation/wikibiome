# Key Claims

- **Periodontitis and T2DM share bidirectional relationship** mediated through oral and gut microbiome
- **Endogenous and microbial biomarkers** can identify individuals at risk for both conditions
- **Oral [[dysbiosis]]** may precede and contribute to systemic metabolic dysfunction

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: Zinc-dependent matrix metalloproteinases drive periodontal tissue destruction
- **[[iron]]**: Iron-requiring oral pathogens (P. gingivalis) link oral [[dysbiosis]] to systemic effects
- **[[gut-metal-microbiome]]**: Oral-gut microbiome axis transmits metal-dependent [[dysbiosis]]
- **[[immune-balance]]**: Periodontal-metabolic immune dysregulation is metal-cofactor dependent
