# Key Findings

- **Chronic periodontitis** associated with increased breast cancer risk via systemic inflammation pathway [[saini-2011-oral-health-breast-cancer-link]], expert-opinion.
- Inflammatory byproducts from oral dysbiosis enter the bloodstream, promoting cellular proliferation and mutagenesis.
- Missing teeth and poor oral hygiene identified as independent risk factors for breast cancer.

## Relevance to WikiBiome

- Connects [[periodontitis]] → systemic inflammation → [[breast-cancer]] pathway.
- Supports the oral-breast translocation hypothesis alongside Söder 2010 and Wu 2022.
