# Key Claims

- **13 gut microbiota taxa and 6 blood metabolites** showed nominal causal association with [[postpartum-depression]] (p < 0.05)
- After Bonferroni correction, **only Bifidobacteriales** (OR: 0.844) showed significant causal protective relationship with lower PPD risk
- **Alphaproteobacteria** (OR: 1.057) retained a strong positive correlation with higher PPD risk after correction
- **Odoribacteron** PPD was mediated indirectly through Hyodeoxycholate (bile acid), with a mediation proportion of 16.8%

# Methods & Findings

- **Design**: Two-sample MR with mediation analysis; 196 GM taxa, 224 blood metabolites; data from IEU Open GWAS and GWAS Catalog
- **Methods**: IVW primary; MR-Egger, weighted median, Cochran's Q, MR-PRESSO for robustness
- **Key implication**: Bile acid-mediated pathway links specific gut microbiota to PPD risk through metabolite intermediaries
