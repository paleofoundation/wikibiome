# Key Claims

- **Postmortem brain tissue** is essential for understanding the biological processes affecting brain biology in ASD
- **Etiological basis** for an individual's autism is rarely determined; underlying mechanisms poorly understood
- **Many genetic variants** associated with ASD involve chromatin remodeling and synapse organization/function
- **Brain is the core organ system** related to ASD; understanding how, where, and when it is affected is crucial
