# Dysbiosis of Gut Microbiota in Patients with Chronic Kidney Disease

## Key Claims

- Butyrate-producing bacteria (Lachnospira, Blautia, Coprococcus, Anaerosti pes, Roseburia) more abundant in controls than [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients
- Lachnospira more abundant in controls than in CKD stage 3a
- Butyrate-producing bacteria deficient from CKD stage 3b onward, including in patients on renal replacement therapy
- Bacterial composition does not recover even after renal replacement therapy

## Methods & Findings

16S rRNA gene sequencing of 93 individuals (73 CKD stage 3-5 + 20 controls). Compared gut microbiome across CKD stages and renal replacement therapy status.
