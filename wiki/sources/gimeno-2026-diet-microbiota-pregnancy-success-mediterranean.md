# Diet-Microbiota Interactions Influence Pregnancy Success

## Key Claims

- CST II-V with Lactobacillus dominance characterized successful pregnancy; all CST IV-B failed
- Mediterranean diet adherence explained greatest proportion of variation in vaginal microbiota structure
