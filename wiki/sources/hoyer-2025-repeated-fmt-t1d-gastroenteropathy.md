# Key Claims

- **Repeated on-demand FMT is effective and safe** for long-term treatment of T1D with severe diabetic gastroenteropathy
- **FMT induced consistent symptom relief** with reduced GSRS-IBS scores across multiple treatments
- **Stool consistency improved** after treatment; Bristol Stool Score 3-5 increased from 28% to 76%
- **Median of 5 FMT treatments per participant** with 5.3 months between treatments
- **Participant satisfaction high (86%)** with 86% reporting considerable benefits

# Methods & Findings

Extended open-label trial from a previous RCT: 17 T1D participants with gastroenteropathy:

- Mean follow-up 33.2 months (range 14.7-39.1 months)
- 95 total FMT treatments administered (primarily oral capsules)
- GSRS-IBS, Bristol Stool Scale, Likert benefit scale assessed
- One serious adverse event possibly related to FMT
