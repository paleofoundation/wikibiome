# TMAO Is Associated with CKD Risk

## Key Claims

- Significant association between TMAO and [[chronic-kidney-disease]] in meta-analysis of 2125 subjects (SMD = -0.93)
- TMAO demonstrates moderate capacity to distinguish CKD from non-CKD (AUC = 0.614)
- Elevated TMAO levels associated with increased CKD risk

## Methods & Findings

Meta-analysis (1240 controls, 885 CKD) plus population-based study of 5584 subjects in China.
