# CKD Prevalence in Population-Based Studies

## Key Claims

- Median [[chronic-kidney-disease]] prevalence 7.2% in persons 30+ years
- CKD prevalence 23.4-35.8% in persons 64+ years
- Prevalence strongly depends on which eGFR equation used

## Methods & Findings

Systematic review of 26 population-based studies using KDOQI definition.
