# Wang 2024 — Gut Microbiota and Fibromyalgia (Mendelian Randomization)

## Key Findings

- **Coprococcus2** causally increases FM risk (OR = 2.317, p = 0.005).
- **Eggerthella** causally increases FM risk (OR = 1.897, p = 0.001) — consistent with meta-analytic enrichment signal.
- **Lactobacillus** (at genus level) causally increases FM risk (OR = 1.576, p = 0.020) — counter-intuitive; likely reflects specific pathobiont species within the genus.
- **Olsenella** and other taxa showed smaller signals.

## Methods

Two-sample MR using MiBioGen microbiome GWAS and FinnGen FM GWAS. Inverse-variance-weighted primary analysis with sensitivity testing (MR-Egger, weighted median).
