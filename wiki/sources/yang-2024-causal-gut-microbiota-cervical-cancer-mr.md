# Causal Correlation Between Gut Microbiota and Cervical Cancer Pathogenesis

## Key Claims

- Higher abundance of class Clostridia and order Clostridiales predicted higher CC risk
- Higher abundance of family Streptococcaceae and genus Dialister predicted lower CC risk
- Mutual causality exists between gut microbiota and cervical cancer
