# CKD and Cognitive Impairment: Systematic Review

## Key Claims

- [[chronic-kidney-disease]] is a significant and independent risk factor for cognitive decline (OR 1.65 cross-sectional; OR 1.39 longitudinal)
- First meta-analysis assessing CKD impact on cognitive decline

## Methods & Findings

Systematic review and meta-analysis of cross-sectional and longitudinal studies (1980-2012). 44 studies, 54,779 participants.
