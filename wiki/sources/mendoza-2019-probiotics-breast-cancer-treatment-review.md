# Potential Effect of Probiotics in the Treatment of Breast Cancer

## Key Claims

- GI bacteria play important role in estrogen modulation, reabsorption, and enterohepatic circulation
- Beta-glucuronidase activity relevant to hormone-driven cancers
- Multiple Lactobacillus strains demonstrate cytotoxic effects toward cancer cells in vitro
