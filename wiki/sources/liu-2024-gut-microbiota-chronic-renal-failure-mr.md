# Causal Relationship between Gut Microbiota and Chronic Renal Failure

## Key Claims

- Ruminococcus gauvreauii protective against chronic renal failure (OR = 0.82)
- Escherichia-Shigella (OR = 1.22) and Howardella (OR = 1.18) are risk factors for chronic renal failure
- After FDR correction, only Escherichia-Shigella and Howardella remain significant

## Methods & Findings

Two-sample MR using MiBioGen and IEU OpenGWAS data. Five MR methods applied with FDR correction.
