# Key Findings

- ***S. gordonii* and *S. mutans* enhance Candida biofilm formation** through glucosyltransferase-mediated extracellular matrix production, creating structurally reinforced polymicrobial communities ([[li-2022-candida-resident-microbiota-interactions]], expert-opinion).
- **Farnesol secreted by Candida induces efflux pump expression in *S. aureus***, conferring enhanced antimicrobial tolerance to the bacterial partner — a direct example of cross-kingdom chemical warfare cooperation.
- **Beta-lactam antibiotics release bacterial peptidoglycan fragments that trigger Candida hyphal growth** — a "peptidoglycan storm" mechanism where antibiotic treatment paradoxically promotes fungal virulence.

Narrative review synthesizing published experimental data on *C. albicans* interactions with commensal and pathogenic bacteria.
