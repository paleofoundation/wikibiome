# Key Claims

- **Autistic individuals had significantly elevated plasma copper and Cu/Zn ratio** and lower (not significantly) plasma Zn compared to controls
- **Zn levels increased significantly** in autistic individuals with and without GI disease after zinc therapy
- **Cu decreased significantly** after zinc therapy in the GI disease group but not in the non-GI group
- **Autistic children with GI disease** significantly improved in hyperactivity and stimming after zinc therapy
- **Zinc therapy may be most effective** at lowering copper levels in autistic children with concurrent GI disease
