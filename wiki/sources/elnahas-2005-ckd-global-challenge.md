# Chronic Kidney Disease: The Global Challenge

## Key Claims

- Number of [[chronic-kidney-disease]] patients threatening to reach epidemic proportions
- Global approach needed from treatment of ESRD to aggressive prevention
- ESRD incidence expected to continue rising 5-8% annually

## Methods & Findings

Seminar examining CKD epidemiology worldwide, risk factors, and strategies for prevention.
