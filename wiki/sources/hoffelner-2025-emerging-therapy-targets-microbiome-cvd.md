# Emerging Therapy Targets to Modulate Microbiome-Mediated Effects Evident in Cardiovascular Disease

## Summary

Review summarizing microbiome-derived metabolites that influence CVD and emerging therapeutic approaches including [[probiotics]], [[prebiotics]], and [[antimicrobial-resistance|antibiotics]] that target microbial diversity and activities to reduce residual CVD risk.

## Key Findings

### Microbiome-Derived Metabolites and CVD
- **SCFAs**: Acetate, propionate, butyrate reduce CVD risk factors; act through OLFR78, GPR41, GPR43 for blood pressure regulation; butyrate strengthens gut barrier
- **TMAO**: Higher levels correlate with more atherosclerosis; interferes with cholesterol transport, foam cell formation, platelet reactivity; linked to cardiac remodeling
- **Bile acids**: Ratio of primary to secondary BAs linked to CVD severity; co-produced bacteria can lower cholesterol
- **Succinate**: Pro-inflammatory signal via SUCNR1; elevated in CVD patients; activates NFkB, ROS, GPRI91
- **Imidazole propionate (ImP)**: Derived from histidine; linked to insulin resistance and T2DM; ImP-producing bacteria increased in CVD patients
- **Tryptophan (Trp)**: Metabolized to indole-3-propionic acid (IPA) and other metabolites; IPA is atheroprotective; *Parabacteroides distasonis* (indole-producing) inversely correlated with plaque size
- **Cholesterol**: Gut microbiota directly converts cholesterol to coprostanol; *Eubacterium* spp. implicated

### Therapeutic Approaches
- **Probiotics**: *Lactobacillus*, *Saccharomyces*, *Enterococcus*, *Bifidobacterium*, *Bacillus*, *Streptococcus*, *Escherichia* used; *L. reuteri* NCIMB 30242 shown to lower cholesterol via BSH activity; *A. muciniphila* reduces permeability and inflammation
- **Prebiotics**: Oligofructose, galacto-oligosaccharides, inulin, polyphenols, PUFAs; inulin-type fructans improve arterial endothelial function in ApoE-/- mice
- **Antibiotics**: Ampicillin, vancomycin, metronidazole shown to modulate cholesterol metabolism and propionate/cholesterol transporter levels; phytosterol reduction also observed

### Clinical Translation (Table 1)
- Several clinical trials initiated for microbiome-targeting CVD approaches
- Challenges include strain specificity, delivery, and long-term maintenance
