# Gut Microbiota Alteration in Chinese CKD Population

## Key Claims

- Total bacteria significantly reduced in ESRD patients
- Prevotella enriched in healthy group while Bacteroides prevalent in ESRD
- Butyrate-producing Roseburia, Faecalibacterium, Coprococcus, Prevotella reduced in ESRD

## Methods & Findings

qPCR and 16S rRNA high-throughput sequencing of 52 ESRD patients and 60 healthy controls in southern China.
