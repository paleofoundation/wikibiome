# Differential Enrichment of Bacteria and Phages in Vaginal Microbiomes in PCOS and Obesity

## Key Claims

- Both obesity and PCOS associated with microbial [[dysbiosis]] but with distinct enrichment patterns
- Lactobacillus bacteria, lactobacillus phage, and pseudomonas phage/virus identified as healthy vaginal microbiome indicators
- Vaginal phage diversity associated with healthy microbiota
