# Key Claims

- **High level of diversity in Firmicutes group (K2)** in both species richness and evenness, consistent with Ruminococcus enterotype
- **Functional enrichment analysis** identified modules associated with degradation of amino acids (arginine, urea) and homoacetogenesis
- **Prediction analysis (Predomics)** suggested potential biomarkers including depletion of Enterococcus faecium and Blautia lineages
- **Enrichment of Absiella spp or Eubacterium limosum** in T2DM individuals highlighted

# Methods & Findings

Nanopore sequencing for metagenomic analysis: 40 T2DM vs 44 controls from UAE:

- DMM enterotyping resulted in 3 dominant groups: Bacteroides (K1), Firmicutes (K2), Prevotella (K3)
- Metagenomic analysis for predicting predisposition to diabetic cardiomyopathy potential demonstrated
- Population-specific microbiome features identified

# Relevance to Metallomic Framework

- **[[zinc]]**: Amino acid degradation pathways enriched in T2DM may reflect zinc-dependent enzyme dysregulation
