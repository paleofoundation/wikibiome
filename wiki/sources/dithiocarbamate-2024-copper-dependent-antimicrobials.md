# Dithiocarbamate 2024 — Copper-Dependent Antimicrobials

## Key Findings
- **Novel dithiocarbamate derivatives require copper to exert antimicrobial effects**, forming copper-drug complexes that are toxic to bacteria [[dithiocarbamate-2024-copper-dependent-antimicrobials]]
- The compounds leverage the host's own copper-based nutritional immunity — they are more effective at infection sites where copper is already elevated
- This approach represents a new paradigm: drugs designed to cooperate with host metal defenses rather than acting independently
