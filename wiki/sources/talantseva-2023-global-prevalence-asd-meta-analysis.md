# Overview

Three-level mixed-effects meta-analysis of ASD prevalence using **85 articles with 317 prevalence estimates** from 29 countries (1994-2019; 120,130,596 individuals). Most comprehensive attempt to address worldwide prevalence with adjustment for multiple moderators.

# Key Findings

## Global Prevalence Estimates

- **ASD overall**: 0.72% (95% CI: 0.61-0.85) = 72 per 10,000
- **Autistic Disorder (AD)**: 0.25% (95% CI: 0.18-0.33)
- **Asperger Syndrome (AS)**: 0.13% (95% CI: 0.07-0.20)
- **AA and PDD-NOS**: 0.18% (95% CI: 0.10-0.28)
- Adjusted final prevalence rate: **1.18%** (95% CI: 0.92-1.48) for 2015-2019 data from Europe and North America

## Increasing Prevalence Over Time

- Global pooled point-prevalence consistently increased from 0.25% (1994-1999) to 0.99% (2015-2019)
- Mean male-to-female ratio: 4.3 (Med = 4.2, SD = 1.5) for ASD overall

## Geographic and Socioeconomic Variation

- **North America**: 1.01% (highest; USA = 1.12%)
- **Europe**: 0.73%
- **Asia**: 0.41%
- Higher-income countries reported higher prevalence, likely reflecting better detection infrastructure
- HDI positively associated with reported prevalence

## Age Effects

- Children aged **6-12 years**: 0.82% (significantly higher than under 5 or over 13)
- Under 5: 0.60%; over 13: 0.57%
