# Key Claims

- **Ketogenic diet significantly modifies gut microbiome composition** in diabetes
- **Metabolic effects of keto diet** include improved glycemic control through microbiome-mediated mechanisms
- **Keto diet shifts microbial community** toward fat-metabolizing organisms
