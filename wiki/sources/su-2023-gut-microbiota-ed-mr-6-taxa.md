# Su 2023 — Six Gut Microbial Taxa Causally Associated with ED (MR)

## Key Findings

- **Six taxa** show causal association with ED:
  - Risk: *Lachnospiraceae* (OR = 1.265, p = 0.008), *Senegalimassilia* (OR = 1.355, p = 0.002), *Tyzzerella 3*, *Oscillibacter*.
  - Protective: *Ruminococcaceae UCG-013* (OR = 0.827, p = 0.049).
- Third independent MR pass over overlapping GWAS data — triangulates the core signature.

## Methods

Two-sample MR with inverse-variance-weighted primary estimator and MR-Egger/weighted-median sensitivity analyses.
