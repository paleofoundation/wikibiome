# Key Claims

- **Cadmium concentration above detection level** in first/second trimester associated with 1.69 (1.08-2.64) times higher ASD risk and 1.29 (0.95-1.75) times higher Non-TD risk
- **Doubling of cesium concentration** marginally associated with 1.89 (0.94-3.80) times higher ASD risk in first/second trimester
- **Third trimester cesium doubling** associated with 1.69 (0.97-2.95) times higher ASD risk
- Meta-analysis of two pregnancy cohorts: **EARLI (n=170) and MARBLES (n=231)** -- siblings of children with ASD
- **Urinary metals measured** using inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry during two pregnancy time periods
