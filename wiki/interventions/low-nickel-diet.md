## Mechanism of Action

A low-nickel diet restricts dietary nickel intake, removing the critical cofactor for multiple virulence enzymes across the endometriosis signature (Primitive 4: Microbial Metal Dependencies as Achilles' Heels).

**Key enzymes disabled by nickel restriction:**

**Nickel glyoxalase** — The enzyme that allows pathogens to evade neutrophils in the bloodstream. Without glyoxalase, all glyoxalase-positive pathogens become susceptible to immune responses. This is the most impactful single-metal restriction in the signature [[pendergrass-2026-endometriosis-conference]].

**Nickel urease** — Increases local pH via ammonia and CO₂ production. Disrupts the tightly regulated acidic pH that vaginal Lactobacillus require. Removing nickel restores pH regulation.

**NiFe-hydrogenase** — Iron and nickel dependent enzymes that decrease local oxygen saturation. Nickel restriction partially disables these, reducing the hypoxic niche that strict anaerobes exploit.

**Candida co-aggregate biofilm biomass** — Nickel significantly increases the biomass of co-aggregate biofilms between Candida and pathogenic bacteria. Reducing nickel weakens these biofilms.

## Triangle Evidence

### Condition: [[endometriosis]]

**I → f (Low-nickel diet disables signature features):**
Nickel is a critical cofactor for glyoxalase, urease, and NiFe-hydrogenase — enzymes that enable immune evasion, pH disruption, and hypoxia respectively. Strong mechanistic evidence supports that restricting nickel disables these pathways.

**I → D (Low-nickel diet improves endometriosis):**
Several clinical studies have found that a low-nickel diet shows promise for endometriosis. Researchers involved in these studies have stated they don't fully understand the mechanisms — the microbiome signature framework provides the mechanistic explanation [[pendergrass-2026-endometriosis-conference]].

**f → D (Nickel-dependent features drive endometriosis):**
The features disabled by nickel restriction — immune evasion, hypoxia, pH disruption, biofilm integrity — are hallmark features of the endometriosis microenvironment.

**Status: Validated** — All three triangle edges hold. The framework explains why a dietary intervention works even when the researchers who discovered it couldn't explain the mechanism.

## Practical Guidance

High-nickel foods to restrict: chocolate, nuts (especially cashews, almonds), seeds, legumes, oats, whole wheat, soy products, shellfish, canned foods (nickel leaching), stainless steel cookware.
