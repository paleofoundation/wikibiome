conventional_rationale: "OC patients receiving chemotherapy are immunocompromised; prophylactic or therapeutic antibiotics prescribed for infection prevention/treatment."
why_counterproductive: "Antibiotic-induced dysbiosis increases OC tumor growth AND cisplatin resistance simultaneously. ABX depletes protective indole metabolites (IPA, indoxyl sulfate), enriches cancer stem cells, and disrupts the immune surveillance that the gut microbiome provides against cancer stem cell populations. Separately, ABX abolishes the chemosensitizing effects of microbiome-dependent drugs (Tripterygium glycosides). The gut microbiome is functionally required for chemotherapy efficacy."
