1. Copper in Rheumatoid Arthritis — wiki/entities/copper.md

Conflict: Cu direction in RA serum

  • Earlier work: [1] (2016, cross-sectional, n=122, Taiwan) — Cu elevated in RA, correlated with ESR
  • Superseding: [2] (2023, case-control, n≈99, Pakistan) — Cu significantly lower in RA (P=0.04)

Evidence hierarchy: case-control > cross-sectional → arshad-2023 takes primary position.

Action: Rewrote RA section to lead with arshad-2023 finding; annotated yang-2016 as earlier cross-sectional work with geographic exposure differences. Updated both source summary pages with explicit conflict notes.

2. Copper in Autism Spectrum Disorder — wiki/entities/autism-spectrum-disorder.md

Conflict: Cu level in ASD

  • Earlier work: [3] (cross-sectional, n=181, hair biomarker) — Cu elevated, severity-correlated

Evidence hierarchy: systematic-review-meta-analysis > cross-sectional → lin-2024 null finding takes primary position.

Action: Rewrote Cu subsection to lead with meta-analytic null; annotated zhou-2025 as single-region study with different biomarker matrix. Updated both source summary pages.
